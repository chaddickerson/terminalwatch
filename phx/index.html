TSA Watch: PHX

Last updated: Mar 26, 2026 05:58 AM ET — 23 reports across 3 days

Terminal 3: Wait times were longer than usual on Wednesday Mar 25, 8:37 AM, with TSA taking 29 minutes compared to a typical 14 minutes.

Multiple travelers reported fast-moving security lines today and earlier this week. TSA PreCheck had essentially no wait, with one traveler reporting no wait in TSA Pre and about a 2 minute wait in the regular line (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:01 PM). Wait times reported include 5 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:01 AM), 10 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:16 PM), and 15 minutes total (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:01 PM). One traveler reported clearing TSA in just 3 minutes on Wednesday Mar 25, 11:42 PM. A wife traveling today cleared security in 20 minutes with additional support (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:45 AM).

Earlier in the week, one traveler reported long lines that were moving on Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 9:01 AM, and another described the airport as "a nightmare" over the past weekend, arriving 2 hours early for a domestic flight and barely making it (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:16 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 2 hours early for domestic flights. Current reports show wait times of 5–20 minutes for most travelers, with TSA PreCheck moving significantly faster than regular lines. Terminal 3 experienced elevated wait times earlier in the week, so allow extra time if using that terminal. Traffic into the terminals has been slow due to closed lanes, so factor in time for ground transportation as well.
