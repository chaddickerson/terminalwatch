TSA Watch: PHX

Last updated: Mar 26, 2026 06:36 AM ET — 22 reports across 3 days

Mar 26: 6:02 AM

Today, a traveler reported that TSA PreCheck took approximately 20 minutes with assistance from ICE agents (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:45 AM).

On Wednesday, wait times at PHX were notably short. One traveler cleared TSA in just 3 minutes around 6 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:42 PM). Another reported a 10-minute wait in the TSA line (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 7:20 PM). A traveler in the regular security line experienced approximately a 2-minute wait, while TSA PreCheck had no wait; the airport was packed at the time (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:01 PM). Multiple travelers reported 5-minute waits (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:13 AM; Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:01 AM), and another reported 10 minutes at 10 AM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:16 PM). A traveler with TSA PreCheck reported a smooth, seamless process when ICE and TSA agents worked together at the checkpoint (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:56 AM). Overall, Wednesday saw manageable security lines (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:34 AM).

Earlier in the week, on Monday, conditions were more challenging. One traveler reported that lines were long but moving, though no ICE agents were visible at the TSA checkpoint, and traffic into the terminals was slow due to closed lanes; this traveler recommended planning ahead (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 9:01 AM). Another traveler who flew over the weekend found conditions at Sky Harbor to be a nightmare and barely made a domestic flight despite arriving 2 hours early (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:16 AM).

Based on recent reports, plan to arrive 20–30 minutes before security screening if you have TSA PreCheck or are traveling when ICE agents are on-site (typically midday onward), as wait times have been minimal to very short. For regular security lines during busy periods, allow closer to 30–45 minutes, though recent reports suggest even regular lines are moving quickly. If you're traveling very early in the morning (before 6:30 AM), expect potentially longer lines and allow extra time; conditions may improve later in the day.
