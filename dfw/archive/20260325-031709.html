On Wednesday, March 18, one traveler reported getting through regular TSA in just 12 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:40 PM).

Recommendation: Current conditions at DFW are excellent. TSA PreCheck holders can plan on arriving 30–45 minutes before their flight for domestic travel. Regular security line passengers should allow 1–1.5 hours, though recent reports suggest even less time may be needed—plan conservatively if you're traveling at peak hours (early morning departures have shown longer waits). Given that PreCheck consistently runs significantly faster than regular lines, it remains a worthwhile investment if you fly frequently.
