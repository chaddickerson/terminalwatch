Recommendation: DFW has been processing travelers smoothly. Arrive 90 minutes early for domestic flights—well ahead of the typical recommendation. TSA PreCheck holders can arrive closer to 60 minutes early given recent wait times under 5 minutes. However, if you're traveling early morning from the East Coast or booking a connection, allow extra buffer time, as one early-morning arrival involved a longer wait.
