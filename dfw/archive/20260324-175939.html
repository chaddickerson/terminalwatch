The most recent reports show very short TSA wait times at DFW. As of mid-morning Tuesday (Tue Mar 24, 10:17 AM), one traveler reported just one person ahead in the general line. Wait times have improved significantly from the weekend. Arrive 1.5–2 hours early for domestic flights at DFW. Current conditions suggest this is more than sufficient for general security lines, and TSA PreCheck holders can plan on minimal delays. If you have flexibility, early morning departures (before 8 AM) appear to offer the fastest processing. Check which terminal your airline uses, as some experience heavier traffic than others.
