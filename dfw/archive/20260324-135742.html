TSA wait times at DFW have been notably short in recent reports. For your DFW departure, plan to arrive 2 hours early for a domestic flight. Based on current conditions, you should clear security well within an hour. If you have TSA PreCheck, you can confidently arrive closer to 90 minutes before departure.
