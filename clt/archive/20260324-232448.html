TSA Watch: CLT

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 06:28 PM ET — 24 reports across 3 days

Mar 24: 6:27 PM|5:25 PM|4:27 PM|3:35 PM|3:21 PM|2:34 PM

TSA screening at CLT today has been exceptionally fast across all lines. A traveler passed through security in less than 3 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:14 PM), and another reported their brother cleared TSA in 2 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 4:41 PM). A separate report noted wait times of 5 minutes for both regular and PreCheck lines (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:22 AM).

Recent reports from the past week show consistently minimal wait times. One traveler waited 6 minutes on Sunday night (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:42 PM). Multiple passengers reported clearing security in under 5 minutes on various recent trips, with some experiencing sub-3-minute passages (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 2:54 PM). PreCheck specifically was reported at 4 minutes and 60 seconds in separate recent visits (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 2:01 PM and Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 12:22 PM).

One outlier report from Monday evening indicated wait times of 48 minutes average across all lines, significantly above the typical 12 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 8:19 PM). However, this appears to have been a brief spike, as reports before and after show normal conditions.

Note that one traveler reported PreCheck was closed at CLT on Friday night, though security still moved faster than usual (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:13 AM).

Recommendation: CLT TSA lines are moving exceptionally fast. Arriving 60–90 minutes before domestic departure should be sufficient for most travelers. PreCheck offers no meaningful advantage given current conditions, as regular lines are processing passengers in minutes. If you're checking bags or connecting from an international flight, allow extra time for those processes rather than TSA screening.
