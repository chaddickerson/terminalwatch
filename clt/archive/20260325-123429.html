This spike does not appear reflected in traveler reports from the same day, which continued to show short waits.

One traveler reported that PreCheck was closed at CLT on Friday night, but security moved faster than usual with no delays (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:13 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive 30–45 minutes before your domestic flight departure at CLT. Both regular and PreCheck lines have been consistently fast over the past week, typically under 10 minutes. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, you can safely arrive closer to 30 minutes before departure. Regular security lines should clear in 5–15 minutes under normal conditions, though the Monday evening spike suggests arriving with extra buffer time during peak travel periods is wise.
