TSA wait times at CLT have been exceptionally short throughout this week. Today, a traveler reported total time from entering the airport to clearing security was 15 minutes, with TSA screening itself taking only a few seconds (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:20 PM). Another traveler got through TSA in 30 seconds (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:19 PM), and a third cleared security in under 10 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:08 PM).

On Wednesday morning, TSA wait was just 3 minutes, with one traveler noting the Starbucks line was longer than the security line (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:55 AM). The airport was notably uncrowded overall (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:01 AM).

Throughout Tuesday and earlier this week, wait times remained minimal. Travelers reported clearing TSA in 2 to 3 minutes at various times (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 4:41 PM; Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:14 PM; Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:22 AM). One report noted 5 minutes for both regular and TSA PreCheck lines (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:22 AM). A Monday morning traveler with a 7:30 AM flight experienced no trouble at TSA (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:36 AM), and another Monday traveler walked right in at 9:30 AM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:10 PM).

One official TSA alert from Monday evening reported longer-than-usual waits, with an average of 48 minutes across all lines (compared to a typical 12 minutes) (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 8:19 PM), but subsequent traveler reports suggest conditions have improved significantly since then.

Recommendation: Based on current conditions, arriving 45 minutes to 1 hour before your domestic flight should be sufficient at CLT. Wait times have been remarkably short across the board—both PreCheck and regular lines are moving very quickly. If Monday evening's alert indicated temporary delays, those do not appear to be ongoing based on traveler feedback through today.
