TSA Watch: CLT

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 03:22 PM ET — 26 reports across 3 days

Mar 24: 3:21 PM|2:34 PM

TSA PreCheck lines at CLT have been exceptionally fast today. There was no wait in the TSA PreCheck line around 11am (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:21 AM). A traveler also reported that both regular and PreCheck lines were moving at 5 minutes today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 07:47 AM).

General reports from earlier today and yesterday continue to show minimal wait times across CLT. A traveler noted no wait in the regular TSA line as of Sunday night (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 02:42 PM). One report from Monday evening indicated TSA wait times were longer than usual, with an average of 48 minutes across all lines compared to a typical 12 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 08:19 PM), but this appears to be an outlier — the vast majority of recent reports show waits under 10 minutes. Multiple travelers over the past week reported clearing security in under 5 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 02:54 PM, Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 01:07 PM), and PreCheck in as little as 60 seconds (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 12:22 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 15–20 minutes before your security screening time — substantially less than the standard recommendation. CLT's PreCheck and regular lines are currently moving very efficiently. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, you can plan for an even tighter buffer, as those lines have consistently shown waits under 5 minutes.
