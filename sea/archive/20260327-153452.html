Airport Update: SEA

Security wait times at SEA remain manageable today. Checkpoint 2 reported 25 minutes in standard lanes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:13 AM), though this report is from yesterday.

A traveler who departed SEA yesterday noted exceptionally light traffic, with only about 10 people in the TSA line (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 11:09 AM). Recent reports indicate SEA has maintained shorter waits than many major U.S. airports during the current operational period. The Port of Seattle's support efforts—including food and monetary assistance for TSA staff—along with private contractors handling preliminary screening tasks, have helped keep lines moving efficiently. Staff morale has reportedly remained positive despite challenges.

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 30–45 minutes before your domestic flight for standard security lanes. Wait times at SEA are notably shorter than national averages, so earlier arrival windows at other major airports may not be necessary here. If you hold PreCheck or CLEAR, expect even faster processing.
