Airport Update: SEA

SEA security checkpoints experienced minimal wait times today. A traveler reported spending a combined 10 minutes in TSA PreCheck lines for a round trip to Orlando (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 1:50 PM). Another passenger returning from Denver cleared security in less than 10 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 12:51 PM). A third report noted that PreCheck and CLEAR lines had no issues and moved quickly, while regular lines were noticeably longer (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 2:54 PM).

Yesterday, TSA PreCheck saw minimal wait times as well. One traveler reported wait times were minimal when using PreCheck (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 2:46 PM). The regular security line had approximately 50 people, while the touchless line was essentially empty (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 1:34 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 1.5 hours early for domestic flights. TSA PreCheck and CLEAR members can arrive closer to 1 hour early, as these expedited lines are moving significantly faster than regular security.
