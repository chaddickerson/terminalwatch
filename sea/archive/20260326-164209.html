Airport Update: SEA

At Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA), Checkpoint 2 reported a wait time of 25 minutes in standard security lanes today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:13 AM). This represents a slight increase from earlier reports but remains manageable for travelers.

Overall, SEA continues to experience relatively normal operations despite ongoing staffing challenges. TSA PreCheck lines have been notably faster, with one traveler reporting a 4-minute wait through the PreCheck lane (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 3:37 PM). The Port of Seattle has provided support to TSA employees working without pay, which has helped maintain service levels.

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 45 minutes to 1 hour before your domestic flight departure. If you have TSA PreCheck, you can likely reduce that to 30–40 minutes given the significantly faster processing in those dedicated lanes. Standard security lines are moving steadily, but today's 25-minute wait at Checkpoint 2 suggests moderate afternoon traffic.
