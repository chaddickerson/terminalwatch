Airport Update: SEA

TSA checkpoint wait times at Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA) remain normal today. PreCheck lines are moving particularly fast, with one traveler reporting a 3-minute wait (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 2:16 PM). General security lines are averaging below 20 minutes (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 3:42 PM). The airport's continued short wait times reflect support from Port of Seattle leadership, who have assumed non-screening roles and coordinated with airlines and community partners, resulting in only a 10% TSA absence rate (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 6:09 PM).

Arrival recommendations: Plan to arrive at least two hours before domestic flights and three hours before international departures (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 3:42 PM). PreCheck members can expect particularly short waits. If you have a connecting flight with a 2.5-hour layover, allow 20–40 minutes for bag claim, 10–20 minutes for rechecking, and 15–30 minutes for TSA rescreening, plus walking time (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 4:21 PM).
