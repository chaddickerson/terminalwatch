Airport Update: SEA

Security conditions at SEA remain exceptionally light today. A traveler with PreCheck Touchless cleared security in just 25 minutes total from arriving at the terminal, including bag check (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 1:44 PM). The regular line showed minimal congestion with roughly 50 people, while the Touchless PreCheck line was essentially empty (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 1:34 PM).

A recent traveler reported spending only 10–15 minutes total in TSA lines across multiple airports including SEA over the past two weeks (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 1:17 PM). PreCheck specifically is moving very quickly, with one passenger clearing in just 3 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 2:16 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 90 minutes before domestic flights and 2 hours before international departures. PreCheck and Touchless lines are significantly faster than regular security—if you don't have PreCheck, expect slightly longer waits, though lines remain short overall.
