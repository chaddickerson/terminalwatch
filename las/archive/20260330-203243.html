Airport Update: LAS

Today at LAS, TSA wait times have been significantly longer than typical. As of mid-afternoon, average wait times across all security lines were approximately 50 minutes, about 21–22 minutes longer than the usual average of 28–29 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 4:03 PM). However, reports vary widely by terminal and line type.

Terminal 3 (T3) experienced overflow at PreCheck earlier today, though CLEAR had minimal wait (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 1:30 PM). One traveler reported a 2.5-hour wait in regular security after flying from Austin this morning, barely making their flight (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 2:21 PM). Another passenger experienced a smooth experience with a 15-minute wait (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 11:27 AM), while a separate report noted 30 minutes this morning (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 3:47 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive at least 90 minutes early for domestic flights today, especially at Terminal 3. CLEAR membership offers significant advantage with minimal waits; PreCheck lines are congested. Regular security lines are experiencing the most substantial delays.
