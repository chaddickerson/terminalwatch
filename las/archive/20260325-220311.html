General Reports (Non-Terminal Specific)

A traveler reported going through TSA today in less than 10 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 3:25 PM). Another traveler reported a 30-minute wait through TSA today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:41 PM).

On recent trips, travelers reported TSA wait times of approximately 1 minute (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:34 PM) and essentially 0 minutes on the return flight from Las Vegas (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:40 PM).

PreCheck took 2 minutes to get through, while normal lines were "not bad" (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 4:55 PM). On Monday morning, TSA lines were under 10 minutes, with most departing passengers able to walk up for an immediate security check (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 2:01 PM).

Recommendation

Based on recent reports, LAS is moving travelers through security quickly. Plan to arrive 1.5 to 2 hours before departure for domestic flights. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, you can likely plan for significantly less time — reports show 2–10 minute waits for these programs. Standard security lines have also been relatively short, typically under 10–30 minutes, though arriving early remains prudent during peak travel times.
