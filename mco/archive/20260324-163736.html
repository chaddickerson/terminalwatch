TSA Watch: MCO

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 11:41 AM ET — 15 reports across 3 days

Earlier reports: Mar 24, 2026 11:40 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 11:17 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 10:40 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 09:57 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 09:24 AM ET

MCO's TSA security lines are moving efficiently as of Tuesday morning. Reports from (Tue Mar 24, 11:12 AM) show travelers clearing security in as little as 10 minutes, with other accounts from (Tue Mar 24, 10:31 AM) reporting 30-minute waits. Earlier that morning (Tue Mar 24, 8:24 AM), one traveler reported clearing in just 90 seconds. TSA PreCheck holders are experiencing notably faster processing—one traveler got through in 15 seconds (Mon Mar 23, 5:57 PM), while general PreCheck lines have ranged from 10–15 minutes across multiple reports from Monday and Tuesday mornings.

Time-of-day patterns show early morning departures are your best bet. Reports from the early morning hours (Mon Mar 23, 7:00 AM and Tue Mar 24, 6:55–8:24 AM) consistently show wait times under 15 minutes, with some as low as 90 seconds to 5 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 6:55 AM). By late afternoon and evening, waits remain reasonable—even at 10 PM (Mon Mar 23, 10:46 PM), one traveler reported a 10-minute wait. The one notable exception was Sunday afternoon (Sun Mar 22, 4:42 PM), when spring break return traffic pushed wait times to around 30 minutes.

The new facial ID program appears to be contributing to faster processing. MCO also offers a dedicated TSA Family line for passengers traveling with children under 12, which averaged 10 minutes (Mon Mar 23, 10:50 AM). Travelers using CLEAR in combination with PreCheck saw even faster results—one family of four got through in under 15 minutes (Mon Mar 23, 8:36 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 1.5–2 hours before departure as normal. If traveling early morning (before 9 AM), you may clear security in well under 15 minutes. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, plan for even faster processing. Avoid Sunday afternoon if possible, as spring break traffic can push waits toward 30 minutes.
