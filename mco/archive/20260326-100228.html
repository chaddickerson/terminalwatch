Travelers have noted that making a security reservation can result in times even quicker than TSA PreCheck (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 11:37 AM).

Recommendation: Current conditions at MCO show highly variable wait times. Plan to arrive at least 90 minutes before domestic departure if using the regular line, particularly given today's morning report of 29 minutes at Terminal West. PreCheck and CLEAR users should generally plan for 15–20 minutes, though recent reports show these lines often move much faster—sometimes in under 5 minutes. If you have the option, a security reservation may offer the fastest passage. Avoid peak morning hours (9–11 AM) if possible, as congestion may develop even when individual line times are short.
