Airport Update: MCO

Today at MCO, wait times have been remarkably short across the airport. Terminal West Standard reported 29 minutes early this morning, but subsequent reports show dramatically faster screening. Terminal C saw a 4-minute TSA wait, and PreCheck cleared in 0 minutes earlier today.

Multiple travelers reported minimal delays throughout the day. One passenger with United, CLEAR, and TSA PreCheck made it through in 6 minutes. Regular TSA lines were actually shorter than PreCheck at times. Evening reports confirmed the trend: a 10-minute security wait and a 3-minute passage through TSA.

Arrive 45 minutes before departure if using regular security. PreCheck and CLEAR holders can arrive closer to their flight time, as both lines moved exceptionally fast today. Terminal C appears particularly efficient, though all terminals are currently operating with minimal waits.
