Airport Update: MCO

Today at MCO, wait times have been notably brief across the airport. A traveler cleared TSA in less than 5 minutes at 3:34 PM (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 3:34 PM). Earlier today, TSA PreCheck moved through in under 2 minutes during the morning (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 1:52 PM), and check-in plus TSA took a combined 45 minutes at 6:51 AM (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:51 AM).

CLEAR membership has delivered exceptional results: one traveler reported going straight through in about 8 minutes with CLEAR while the regular TSA line stretched a quarter-mile deep (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 3:23 PM). TSA PreCheck holders consistently reported minutes-long waits, while travelers without either credential experienced 20–40 minute standard line times today (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 2:26 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 1.5–2 hours early if you lack PreCheck or CLEAR. With either credential, 1 hour should suffice. Standard security lines are notably slower than expedited options.
