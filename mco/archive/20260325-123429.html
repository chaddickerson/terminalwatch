Standard security lines appear to be moving in 10–30 minutes, while TSA PreCheck and CLEAR are processing travelers significantly faster—often in just a few minutes. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, you can arrive slightly closer to your departure time. Terminal C has been noted as running especially smoothly.
