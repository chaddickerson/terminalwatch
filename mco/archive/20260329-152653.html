Airport Update: MCO

At MCO's South gates (C230–C249), wait times reached approximately 35 minutes in regular security lines earlier today (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:09 AM). PreCheck lines remained under 10 minutes across all gates. In contrast, West gates (1–59) and East gates (70–129) experienced significantly shorter waits of approximately 15 minutes in standard lines (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:09 AM).

Earlier this morning, Terminal East standard screening reported 32-minute waits (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 3:52 AM), while Terminal West standard lines ran significantly longer at 56 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 3:52 AM). A traveler with CLEAR and PreCheck experienced less than 5 minutes through security, and even regular lines were under 10 minutes (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 8:17 AM).

Recommendation: If flying from MCO today, arrive 60–75 minutes early if using standard security at South gates, but West and East gates should require only 30–45 minutes. PreCheck and CLEAR holders can plan for significantly shorter screening times across all terminals.
