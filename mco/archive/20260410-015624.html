At Terminal A today, CLEAR members moved through security in approximately 10 minutes, while the regular TSA line had about a 40-minute wait (Twitter - Thu Apr 09, 7:23 AM).

For your arrival planning at MCO today, CLEAR provides a significant advantage—especially at Terminal A where regular lines are running considerably longer. Note that ground transportation (shuttles) may be crowded. Arrive at least 90 minutes early for domestic flights if using standard security; 60 minutes should suffice with CLEAR.
