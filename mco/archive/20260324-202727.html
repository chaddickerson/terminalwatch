A traveler using PreCheck at the JetBlue terminal got through security in 15 seconds (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 5:57 PM). Another report from the JetBlue terminal today noted no wait at all, with praise for the friendly staff and terminal amenities (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:44 AM).

Across MCO today, wait times have been consistently quick. One traveler got through TSA in just 10 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:31 AM). Another reported 12 minutes from start to finish (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:32 PM). Additional reports from earlier today include one traveler who went through in 90 seconds (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:24 AM), and another who reported standard wait times of 30–40 minutes for check-in and security combined (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:07 PM). A traveler returning yesterday from Italy noted breeze-through conditions, with their only delay caused by packing prohibited items (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:09 PM).

Yesterday evening, a traveler with a family of 4 using CLEAR and TSA PreCheck got through security in less than 15 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 8:36 PM). Another got through in 10 minutes late Monday night (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 10:46 PM). A PreCheck traveler cleared in 10 minutes at 7 a.m. on Monday (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 12:57 PM). One report noted getting through in less than 5 minutes around 12:30 p.m. on Monday (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 12:37 PM).

Earlier Monday morning, a PreCheck traveler estimated roughly 10 minutes at around 5 a.m., with general security taking longer (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 5:36 AM). Sunday afternoon saw 30 minutes of wait time to reach TSA, attributed to Spring Break return traffic (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 4:42 PM). One traveler reported that making a reservation for general security can actually be faster than TSA PreCheck (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 11:37 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 30–45 minutes before your flight for standard security. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, 15–20 minutes should generally suffice, though today's data shows many PreCheck users clearing in 10 minutes or less. The JetBlue terminal appears notably fast. Since MCO has implemented facial ID technology, processing is moving smoothly. However, if you're traveling on a Sunday during Spring Break, add extra buffer time—Sunday afternoon saw 30-minute waits to reach the security checkpoint itself.
