MCO security wait times have been light in recent days. On Monday, March 23 at 9:49 AM, travelers reported exceptionally short lines, with one passenger noting they could have slept longer. The pattern suggests early morning arrivals (before 9 AM) and mid-to-late morning slots offer the shortest waits at MCO. If traveling on a weekend during Spring Break season, expect significantly longer lines in afternoon hours. Plan to arrive at least 90 minutes before domestic flights during peak afternoon times, or 60 minutes if departing in early morning hours.
