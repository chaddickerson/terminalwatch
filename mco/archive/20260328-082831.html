Airport Update: MCO

Yesterday morning, TSA wait times at MCO were generally quick. A traveler flying Southwest reported 20–30 minutes max in the security line (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 6:11 AM), while another passenger got through in just 10 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 6:17 AM). Later that afternoon, TSA screening was notably fast, with one traveler waiting only 10 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:58 AM). By evening, security moved so quickly that one passenger said it took less time than getting into the Delta lounge (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 5:36 PM).

For travelers using expedited options, results were even faster. A passenger with United, CLEAR, and TSA PreCheck cleared security in just 6 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:00 PM), and another reported a 3-minute wait (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 1:25 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 45 minutes before a domestic flight. Terminal C reported very manageable times, though all terminals showed quick processing yesterday. If you have CLEAR or TSA PreCheck, you can safely arrive closer to your flight time.
