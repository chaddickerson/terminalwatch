Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 09:59 AM ET — 14 reports across 3 days

As of Tuesday morning (Mar 24, 8:24 AM), MCO is operating efficiently with wait times under 90 seconds through standard security lanes (Tue Mar 24, 8:24 AM, 6:55 AM, and 7:47 AM). Multiple travelers report clearing TSA in single-digit to low double-digit minutes across the airport, even during the spring break travel period.

TSA PreCheck lines are consistently fast, with several reports of 10–15 minute waits or less (Mon Mar 23, 5:57 PM, 12:57 PM; Tue Mar 24, 7:47 AM). Standard security saw longer delays over the weekend into Monday afternoon—Terminal South Standard peaked at 30 minutes on Monday (Mon Mar 23, 1:23 PM)—but has since returned to normal. Early morning departures (5–7 AM) show the shortest queues (Sun Mar 22, 5:36 AM; Mon Mar 23, 12:57 PM).

Arrive 45 minutes before departure for PreCheck or early morning travel; plan 60–75 minutes for standard security during mid-day hours. MCO has a TSA Family line for children under 12, which can move faster during peak times. The airport is currently well-staffed and running smoothly despite spring break volume.
