TSA security lines at Denver International Airport (DEN) are moving exceptionally fast. Today, security lines were reported at approximately 12 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:21 PM). A traveler noted that TSA officers at DEN were incredibly friendly and efficient (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 10:43 AM).

Recent reports from the past day show consistently short wait times. Yesterday, a traveler using regular (non-PreCheck) security at DEN encountered no line at all and noted staffing was great (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:48 PM). TSA PreCheck wait times yesterday were approximately 4 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 4:34 PM). A traveler using CLEAR reported less than 3 minutes end-to-end at DEN yesterday (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:07 PM). Other reports from yesterday included wait times of 5 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:29 PM) and 10 minutes for regular security (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:26 PM). Earlier yesterday morning, security lines were under 15 minutes despite higher spring break volume (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:51 AM), and the DEN west gate checkpoint processed a traveler in 2 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:47 AM).

Over the past several days, wait times have remained consistently short. On Monday, DEN has been reported with wait times of approximately 3 minutes (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 4:25 PM), 10 minutes during spring break travel (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 3:25 PM), and under normal operating times (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 12:03 PM). Earlier in the week, wait times ranged from 20–30 minutes during one traveler's experience (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 2:16 PM).

DEN is also reported to offer a time-reservation system that allows travelers to book TSA screening in 15-minute increments and proceed to the front of the line without PreCheck (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:32 PM).

Recommendation: Arriving 15–20 minutes before your screening should be sufficient at DEN based on current conditions. If you have CLEAR, expect to be processed in under 3 minutes. PreCheck travelers are seeing 4–10 minute waits, while regular security lines are typically under 15 minutes. The airport's efficiency has been consistently strong even during spring break travel, so DEN is currently one of the faster major airports for TSA screening.
