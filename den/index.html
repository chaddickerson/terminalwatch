Earlier data from the past week shows consistent short waits. Recommendation: Plan to arrive 1–1.5 hours before departure for domestic flights. Based on recent reports, TSA lines at DEN have been consistently fast—most travelers are clearing security in 5–15 minutes, and several report even quicker times. The west gate checkpoint appears particularly efficient. Even with Spring Break volumes, lines remain manageable. Standard TSA PreCheck and CLEAR benefits should apply, though recent reports do not specify separate line times for these programs.
