Airport Update: DEN

Denver's TSA security lines are moving very quickly today. The West checkpoint reported wait times of 25–29 minutes in standard lanes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:13 AM), while the East checkpoint showed 35+ minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:13 AM). More recent individual reports paint an even rosier picture: one traveler experienced only 5 minutes to clear security (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 1:52 PM), another reported the line was shorter than the McDonald's queue (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:23 AM), and a PreCheck traveler noted just 2 minutes (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 6:58 AM).

Plan to arrive 30–45 minutes before your flight if using standard security; PreCheck holders should plan for just 10–15 minutes. West checkpoint lines appear slightly faster than East today, so consider that when choosing your route through the airport.
