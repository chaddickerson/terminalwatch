Airport Update: DEN

TSA wait times at Denver Airport (DEN) today are running longer than typical. As of around 11 AM ET, average wait times across all security lines were approximately 21 minutes, compared to a typical average of 5 minutes—a difference of about 16 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 11:03 AM). However, a broader airport report noted that at roughly the same time, DEN was among only a few major U.S. airports reporting waits of 35 minutes or longer, with most of the top 20 airports seeing waits under 20 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 11:06 AM).

For travelers heading to DEN today, plan to arrive at least 30–40 minutes early for domestic flights to account for current delays. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, those expedited lanes should help you move through faster, though specific line times for those services were not reported today. Standard security lines appear to be the primary bottleneck at this time.
