TSA Watch: LGA (focus: Terminal C)

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 08:51 AM ET — 6 reports across 3 days

Expect significant delays at LGA Terminal C right now. Standard TSA lines are running 2–3 hours (Sun Mar 22, 6:37 AM) with reports of waits stretching to 3 hours (Mon Mar 23, 6:41 PM). Even TSA PreCheck is heavily congested, with hundred-person lines that are moving but extremely backed up (Tue Mar 24, 11:23 AM). One traveler reported 20 minutes for PreCheck at the American Airlines terminal (Sun Mar 22, 5:51 PM), suggesting some variation by gate area, but this appears to be the exception rather than the rule.

Early morning appears to be the worst time. A traveler departing at 6:00 AM encountered what they described as a 2–3 hour line (Sun Mar 22, 8:37 AM). Evening waits also remain severe, with 2-hour standard lines reported (Sun Mar 22, 10:20 PM). The busiest reported time was mid-to-late morning on Tuesday (Tue Mar 24, 11:23 AM), when PreCheck lines were described as "hundreds long."

Arrive at the airport at least 3–4 hours before departure if you don't have PreCheck, and 2.5–3 hours if you do. Even with PreCheck, expect substantial delays. If your ticket doesn't show PreCheck status, verify it in advance to avoid surprises at security.
