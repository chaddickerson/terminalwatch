One report mentioned snaking lines at an international connecting flight checkpoint, though circumstances were not typical of standard departures (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 5:51 AM).

Recommendation: Based on today's data, PreCheck and CLEAR members should plan to arrive 30–45 minutes before domestic departure times; regular passengers should aim for 1.5–2 hours early. PreCheck and touchless options are significantly faster—often clearing in under 5 minutes—so enrolling in these programs is highly worthwhile if you travel frequently from MIA. Current staffing appears adequate, and wait times are well below historical peaks.
