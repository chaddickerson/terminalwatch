Airport Update: MIA

Early this morning at MIA, TSA PreCheck lines were moving exceptionally fast. At 8:20 AM EDT, PreCheck wait times were under 5 minutes across all checkpoints, while standard security lines were under 15 minutes (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:10 AM). By mid-morning, conditions remained favorable: a traveler at H Gate around 10 AM reported breezed-through security with no lines (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:48 AM), and another traveler cleared both customs and security in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 12:14 PM).

However, early-morning arrivals faced challenges. At 5 AM, TSA had just opened and lines were already building (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 05:55 AM). Forecast indicates spring break traffic will increase wait times later today.

Recommendation: If traveling today, arrive early (before 10 AM) to take advantage of current short wait times, especially for PreCheck. Afternoon and evening departures will likely see significantly longer lines. Standard security lines may exceed 15 minutes as the day progresses.
