Airport Update: MIA

Today at MIA, security lines were exceptionally short. One traveler reported walking right up to the TSA agent with virtually no wait (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 11:32 AM), and another confirmed a 5-minute wait earlier today (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 11:08 AM).

Terminal E: Early this morning, TSA PreCheck with touchless ID took just 1 minute, while CLEAR processed passengers in about 3 minutes (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 7:15 AM).

For your visit, plan to arrive 1 to 1.5 hours before your boarding time. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, expect minimal delays—under 5 minutes based on today's reports. Regular security lines also appear to be moving efficiently at present, though PreCheck and CLEAR will be faster. Saturday afternoon conditions appear favorable, but arriving early remains the safest strategy for any flight.
