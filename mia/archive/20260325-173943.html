Wait times at MIA today have been exceptionally short. A traveler reported 1 minute through Checkpoint 8 (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:12 PM). Earlier today, another traveler passed through TSA in less than 10 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:17 AM), and a third reported zero TSA delays with no overcrowding (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:01 AM). However, one traveler experienced a 27-minute wait at 4:10 a.m. this morning (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:06 AM).

PreCheck and expedited options have been moving particularly fast. TSA PreCheck was reported as only a short wait on Tuesday evening (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:33 AM), and on Monday evening, a traveler with PreCheck and Real ID Touchless cleared security in under 5 minutes while the regular line was approximately 15–20 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 8:12 PM). CLEAR has also shown strong performance: one traveler waited just 5 minutes with CLEAR while the regular line was about 25 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 6:22 PM).

Recent reports from earlier this week confirm the trend. On Tuesday, a traveler cleared TSA Pre in just 3 minutes from drop-off (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:00 PM), and another reported 6 minutes through TSA at 9 a.m. on March 23 (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:17 AM). On Monday at 6:30 p.m., PreCheck and Real ID Touchless took under 5 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 8:12 PM), and CLEAR alone was 5 minutes while regular lines ran 25 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 6:22 PM). On Sunday, a traveler with Global Entry waited about 20 minutes to clear (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 3:27 PM).

One traveler arriving very early reported that TSA opened at 3:45 a.m. for a 5:30 a.m. flight but passengers were not able to process until close to departure time (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:56 PM).

Recommendation: Based on current data, plan to arrive 1–2 hours early for domestic flights at MIA. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, you can likely arrive closer to the 1-hour mark, as those lines are consistently moving in under 10 minutes. Regular security lines are typically running 15–28 minutes, so standard passengers should allow the full 2 hours to account for check-in. Note that very early morning departures (before 5 a.m.) may have limited TSA staffing availability, so give yourself extra buffer time if flying at that hour.
