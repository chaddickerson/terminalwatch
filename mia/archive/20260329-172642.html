Airport Update: MIA

At Miami International Airport (MIA) today, security screening times vary significantly by time of day. Early this morning at 8:20 AM, standard TSA lines were less than 15 minutes and PreCheck lines were just 5 minutes across all checkpoints (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:10 AM). By mid-morning at 10:48 AM, travelers at H Gate reported no lines and breezed through quickly (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:48 AM). Most recently at 12:14 PM, one traveler cleared customs and security in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 12:14 PM).

However, experts caution that wait times are expected to increase later today due to spring break traffic (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:10 AM). Early morning saw longer delays, with reports of significant lines around 5 AM when TSA opened (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 5:55 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive 2–3 hours early for domestic flights today, especially for afternoon departures as spring break congestion builds. PreCheck and CLEAR members should see substantially faster processing based on morning reports.
