TSA security at MIA is currently moving quickly. The most recent report (Tue Mar 24, 09:15 AM) indicates no lines at TSA checkpoints. Arrive 45 minutes to 1 hour before your flight for standard security, or 30–45 minutes if you have PreCheck. Current staffing appears adequate, and spring break congestion has not created the extended delays seen at other major hubs. Allow extra time only if you're checking bags or flying during peak evening hours (5–8 PM), when general lines approach 25 minutes.
