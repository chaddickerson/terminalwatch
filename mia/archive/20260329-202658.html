Airport Update: MIA

At Miami International Airport (MIA) today, security wait times have varied significantly throughout the day. Early this morning at 8:20 AM, standard security lines were under 15 minutes while PreCheck was under 5 minutes across all checkpoints (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:10 AM). By mid-morning around 10 AM at the H Gate checkpoint, there were no lines and TSA processing was very quick (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:48 AM). A traveler who cleared customs and security reported it took just 5 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 12:14 PM). However, at 5 AM when the airport opened, lines were already building (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 5:55 AM). Generally, wait times have been short today, though experts note spring break traffic may cause delays later in the afternoon and evening.

Recommendation: If traveling from MIA today, arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights. PreCheck lines appear significantly faster (5 minutes versus 15 minutes for standard lines), so consider expedited screening if available. Avoid peak afternoon and evening hours if possible.
