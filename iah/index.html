Terminal E was experiencing extreme delays today. At 6:56 PM ET, wait times reached 232 minutes — a staggering 194 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 06:56 PM).

Across all terminals at IAH today, conditions have been severely congested. The average wait time across all security lines reached 173 minutes — 162 minutes above normal — as of 6:05 PM ET (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 06:05 PM). Individual reports from travelers today documented waits of approximately 3–4.5 hours (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 05:12 PM). However, some travelers reported faster processing — one cleared in approximately 45 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 05:36 PM), and another in 1 hour 30 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 07:10 PM).
