Airport Update: IAH

Today at Terminal E, wait times have been severely elevated throughout the morning. At 8:30 AM, Terminal E reported 199 minutes compared to a typical 51 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 8:30 AM). Earlier at 5:49 AM, the same terminal showed 232 minutes versus a typical 25 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:49 AM).

At Terminal A, conditions have been similarly difficult. At 5:49 AM today, wait times reached 232 minutes compared to a typical 34 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:49 AM). A traveler reported that at 3:05 AM, both north and south checkpoints in Terminal A were operating, but the lines extended past baggage claim, down the escalator to the basement toward Terminal B, and continued to grow longer as the morning progressed (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:07 AM). Another traveler who arrived at Terminal A at 6:08 AM reported taking 2.5 hours total from airport entry to reaching their gate, arriving at the gate 50 minutes before boarding (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:08 AM).

Terminal B experienced significant crowding today. A traveler who got in line at 3:05 AM cleared security by 5:13 AM (approximately 2 hours), but noted that lines were getting substantially longer as time progressed and snaked past baggage claim down to the basement (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:07 AM).

Across all terminals today, average wait times have been extraordinary. At 8:27 AM, the airport-wide average reached 199 minutes versus a typical 30 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 8:27 AM). At 6:24 AM, the average peaked at 264 minutes compared to a typical 31 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:24 AM). Multiple travelers reported extremely long waits: one passenger waited 1 hour 40 minutes in the security line and missed their flight (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 8:55 AM), and another reported security lines were nearly out the doors at 6 AM with wait times exceeding 1 hour (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:40 AM).

Yesterday (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 2:30 PM), IAH posted three- to four-hour wait times Wednesday morning—the third consecutive day of severe delays. A traveler reported waiting over 6 hours to get through security (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 9:41 AM). On Tuesday (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 11:40 AM), a traveler arrived at 4:30 AM and cleared security by 6:23 AM, but by that time travelers still in line faced a minimum 4-hour wait. At Terminal E on Monday (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 10:35 AM), a TSA PreCheck traveler waited 90 minutes after arriving 3.5 hours before their flight at 4 AM, though an additional security lane opening later helped speed processing.

Recommendation: Arrive at least 4–5 hours before your departure time today. Only Terminal A and E are currently operating. Regular security lines are experiencing extreme delays of 3+ hours; the data does not yet distinguish PreCheck or CLEAR wait times today, though early in the week TSA PreCheck offered some advantage. Given the severity and unpredictability of current conditions, arriving as early as possible—ideally when the airport opens or shortly after—provides the best chance of making your flight.
