TSA Watch: IAH

Last updated: Mar 26, 2026 06:34 AM ET — 6 reports across 2 days

Mar 26: 6:02 AM

Early this morning, IAH experienced severe TSA congestion. A traveler reported 2 hours of wait time to reach the TSA checkpoint area (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:50 AM). Another traveler documented the line stretching downstairs and through a long underground tunnel at 3:39 AM, raising concerns about making a 6:00 AM flight (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:47 AM). An earlier report from 4:24 AM noted that only 2 TSA terminals were open with the security line extending all the way down to the subway and nearly the full length of the underground tunnel, despite passengers arriving 4 hours before their flights (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:24 AM).

On Wednesday, reports varied considerably. One traveler completed TSA screening in 1 hour without incident (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:36 AM). However, another traveler reported a 3-hour TSA wait on the return journey from IAH (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 3:16 PM).

Recommendation: Based on today's severe early-morning delays, plan to arrive at least 3–4 hours early for morning flights. The data does not break out performance by specific terminals or PreCheck/CLEAR versus regular lines, but the consistent reports of extensive queues suggest all checkpoint types experienced delays this morning. If flying from IAH today, expect significant wait times regardless of your security lane type.
