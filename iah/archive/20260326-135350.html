Airport Update: IAH

TSA wait times at IAH today remain critically long. Terminal E reported 199 minutes today at 8:30 AM, nearly four times the typical 51-minute wait (Thu Mar 26, 8:30 AM). Terminal A faced 4-hour waits early this morning, with one traveler reporting they entered security at 3:05 AM and cleared by 5:13 AM, with lines visibly worsening as morning progressed (Thu Mar 26, 7:07 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive at least 4–5 hours before departure today. Only Terminals A and E currently have open security. PreCheck access is unavailable, so all passengers face the same extended lines.
