Airport Update: IAH

Severe TSA delays continue at IAH today. Terminal A reported wait times of 232 minutes as of 8:28 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:28 AM), with earlier reports showing 264 minutes at 5:44 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 5:44 AM). The security line extended underground from Terminal A to the Marriott (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:03 AM). Terminal E showed 232 minutes at 9:45 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:38 AM). Terminal C offered a faster alternative: United status holders (Global Services, Premier 1K, Platinum, Gold) accessed a dedicated checkpoint with significantly shorter waits—one traveler cleared security in 25 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:38 AM).

Airport-wide, average wait times across all terminals reached 232–248 minutes throughout the morning (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:49 AM). One traveler reported walking 2.4 miles with approximately 90 minutes remaining in line (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 8:53 AM).

Arrive 4+ hours early for standard security lines at IAH today. If you hold elite United status, use Terminal C's dedicated checkpoint for dramatically faster processing. PreCheck and CLEAR lines, where available, will move significantly faster than regular lanes.
