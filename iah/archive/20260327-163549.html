Airport Update: IAH

TSA wait times at IAH remain significantly elevated today. Terminal A is experiencing 132-minute waits, nearly 4 times typical levels (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 12:24 PM). Terminal E reported 100-minute waits, well above its normal 37 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 12:21 PM). Across all terminals, average wait times reached 182 minutes this morning, compared to a typical 35 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:58 AM).

Individual travelers report even longer waits. One passenger waited 2 hours in line after arriving at the airport (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 12:04 PM), while another reported a 4-hour wait in Terminal A (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 10:45 AM). A traveler documented walking 2.4 miles in the security queue with 90 minutes remaining after already waiting 2 hours (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 8:53 AM). The TSA line extends into underground subway corridors and outside the terminal.

Recommendation: Arrive at least 3–4 hours early for domestic flights from IAH today, particularly at Terminals A and E. Consider alternatives like Houston Hobby if possible. No PreCheck or CLEAR advantage data is available in today's reports.
