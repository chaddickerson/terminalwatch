Airport Update: IAH

Terminal A: Posted wait times today reached 264 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:43 PM). However, a traveler who passed through Terminal A earlier today reported clearing security in about 1 hour despite posted times of 180 minutes, noting the line moved constantly (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:50 PM).

Terminal E: Posted wait times reached 199 minutes today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:44 PM).

General airport conditions: Average wait times across all IAH security lines peaked at 232 minutes today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:02 PM). Most recent reports describe disorganized lines with no clear queue structure (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:37 PM). One traveler arriving at 10 AM cleared both baggage and TSA in 2 hours 20 minutes, though noting the line was longer than usual (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:35 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 4+ hours before departure today. Posted times significantly exceed actual wait times—one traveler cleared in roughly half the posted duration—but current conditions remain severely congested. Expect disorganization and prolonged screening regardless of terminal.
