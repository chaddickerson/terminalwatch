Airport Update: IAH

TSA security wait times at IAH today are significantly elevated across the airport. Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:37 PM reports average wait times across all lines at 147 minutes, compared to a typical 33 minutes—a difference of +114 minutes.

Terminal E is experiencing the longest delays. Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:23 PM reports current wait times of 199 minutes in Terminal E, versus a typical 34 minutes. One traveler reported waiting 3+ hours in the Terminal E security line earlier today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:24 PM).

Terminal A showed relatively better performance. One traveler who arrived at 10:50 AM reported getting through in 90 minutes, despite the website showing 190 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 1:26 PM). Another traveler who arrived at 8:22 AM cleared Terminal A security in under 45 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:38 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 4–5 hours before your flight, particularly if traveling through Terminal E. Terminal A appears to have shorter waits. Avoid checked bags if possible to expedite the process.
