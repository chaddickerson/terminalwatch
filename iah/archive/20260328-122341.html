Airport Update: IAH

IAH experienced severe TSA congestion today, with wait times ranging dramatically depending on arrival time. One traveler who arrived at 3:50 AM cleared security by 5:15 AM (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 06:31 AM), while another who arrived at 3:09 AM waited until 4:57 AM (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 06:26 AM). However, reports from later in the morning paint a grimmer picture. One traveler endured 3.5 hours in what they described as exhausting, circular queuing through multiple levels and outside areas (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 07:14 AM). Another noted the line extended from the lower-level subway station in a circle along the track (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 07:09 AM). A traveler arriving early managed just over 1 hour with continuous movement (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 07:01 AM), and another cleared security in about 1 hour (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 06:58 AM).

Arrive 2–3 hours early to ensure sufficient buffer time, particularly during morning peak hours. Very early arrivals (before 6 AM) appear to experience shorter waits of 1–1.5 hours, but those arriving later face significantly longer delays.
