Airport Update: IAH

Terminal E at IAH is experiencing severe delays today. TSA wait times reached 243 minutes (approximately 4 hours) in Terminal E, far exceeding the typical 25-minute wait (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:30 PM). Multiple travelers reported the security line snaking across multiple floors and terminals, with at least 4 hours needed to clear security screening (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:07 PM). Terminal A also showed significant congestion, with security lines extending underneath Terminal C (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:35 PM).

Across all IAH terminals, average wait times today were approximately 3.5 hours (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:01 PM), compared to a typical 19–39 minutes. Earlier today, one traveler reported clearing baggage and TSA in 2 hours 20 minutes after arriving at 10 a.m. (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:35 PM), suggesting conditions may have worsened significantly by afternoon.

Arrive at least 4–5 hours early for departure flights today at IAH. Terminal E is particularly congested; consider arriving even earlier if flying from there. No PreCheck or CLEAR distinctions were reported in today's data.
