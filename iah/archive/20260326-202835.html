Airport Update: IAH

TSA wait times at IAH today remain severely elevated. Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:01 PM reported average waits of 208 minutes across all lines, compared to a typical 21 minutes—a difference of +187 minutes.

Terminal E has been hit particularly hard. The most recent report showed 199-minute waits (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:39 PM). A video posted earlier today documented a single line that winds from the first floor upstairs, outside, and back inside, with travelers facing at least 4 hours to clear security (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:07 PM).

Terminal A posted 180-minute waits around 2:50 PM, though one traveler reported clearing security in approximately 50 minutes when passing through earlier today at 9:00 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:50 PM).

Earlier today, one traveler reported a 1 hour 15 minute wait in the morning (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:55 PM), and another cleared baggage and TSA in 2 hours 20 minutes after arriving at 10:00 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:35 PM).

Arrive at least 4+ hours before your flight, especially for Terminal E. Morning arrivals (before 10 AM) may experience shorter waits than afternoon peak times.
