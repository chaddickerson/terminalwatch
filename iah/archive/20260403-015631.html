Today's data is limited. Earlier today, a report indicated 5-hour security lines at Houston Hobby due to a 55% callout rate among TSA staff (Bluesky - Thu Apr 02, 7:25 AM). However, this refers to Houston Hobby Airport, not IAH.

The most recent IAH-specific reports are from yesterday and earlier in the week. At Terminal C, PreCheck had no line and regular security showed 15–20 minute waits (Twitter - Wed Apr 01, 3:12 PM). Checkpoint E took approximately 25 minutes (Twitter - Wed Apr 01, 8:07 AM). Earlier in the week, wait times at IAH had been significantly longer—reports mentioned 4-hour delays—but conditions improved after TSA staff received back pay.

Recommendation: Arrive 2–3 hours early for domestic flights at IAH. PreCheck and private screening at Terminal C appear faster; if available, these options are worth considering. Monitor conditions before your flight, as staffing levels remain fluid.
