Airport Update: IAH

Terminal A: Early this morning, wait times at Terminal A were significantly elevated, with 100 minutes reported at 6:12 AM (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 6:12 AM). By mid-morning around 10:45 AM, one traveler reported getting through in just 9 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:45 AM). However, earlier reports suggested approximately 2 hours wait time at Terminal A around 5:30 AM, with a recommendation to allow 4 hours if checking a bag (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:06 AM). PreCheck and CLEAR are not currently operating.

Terminal C: Terminal C experienced the longest waits today. A traveler reported ~60 minutes at 8 AM, though the checkpoint was supposed to accommodate UA Premier Access and passengers with disabilities (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:57 AM). Another report indicated approximately 45 minutes with no PreCheck option available, as the former United 1K/Global Services dedicated line opened to all passengers (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 11:02 AM). As of 10 AM, Terminal C waits exceeded 40 minutes (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 11:41 AM).

Terminal E: Terminal E showed mixed results. At 6:08 AM, wait times were 50 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 6:08 AM). By mid-morning, reports suggested ~20 minutes (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 11:41 AM) and ~35 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 1:53 PM).

By early afternoon, conditions improved dramatically across the airport. One traveler reported getting through in less than 15 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 3:10 PM).

Recommendation: Conditions improved significantly as the day progressed. If traveling later today, plan to arrive 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights, though Terminal C may still require extra time. Earlier morning travelers should add an extra hour. Note that PreCheck and CLEAR remain unavailable, and only three of five security checkpoints are operating with limited lanes.
