General reports indicate afternoon and evening conditions improved significantly, with some travelers clearing security in under 15–30 minutes by 3–5 PM (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 4:37 PM). PreCheck and CLEAR remain unavailable (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 10:06 AM).
