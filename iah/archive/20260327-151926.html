Airport Update: IAH

IAH is experiencing severe TSA congestion today. Terminal E reported wait times of 232 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:38 AM), while Terminal A saw 232-minute waits as well (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:28 AM). One traveler in Terminal A reported entering security at 5:15 AM and clearing by 8:05 AM, with about 2 hours in the tunnels but roughly an hour from first signage to the checkpoint (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:01 AM). Terminal C fared better: a United status holder cleared security in 25 minutes through a dedicated United checkpoint (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:38 AM). Airport-wide averages ranged from 182 to 248 minutes across the morning (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:30 AM).

General reports indicate lines extend through basement corridors and subway tunnels, with some stretching outside and back inside the terminal (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:52 AM). One traveler walked 2.4 miles and spent 2+ hours with an estimated 90 minutes remaining in line (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 8:53 AM). Multiple travelers report missing flights (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 10:23 AM).

Arrive at least 4–5 hours early for flights at IAH today. Terminal C may offer faster processing for eligible United members. Avoid tight connections; the airport is running 150+ minutes behind typical security times.
