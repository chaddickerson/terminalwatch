Airport Update: IAH

Wait times at IAH have improved dramatically today. TSA security was under 5 minutes earlier today, and Terminal C reported a 23-minute wait at 8:20 AM. Another traveler cleared IAH in approximately 15 minutes, while Terminal C took 27 minutes at 6:55 AM.

Earlier this morning, conditions were slower. At 8:49 AM, average wait times across all lines were 54 minutes, with Terminal E at 63 minutes. However, Terminal A reported passing through in 26 minutes starting at 7:19 AM.

The dramatic improvement correlates with TSA agents receiving backpay earlier today. Wait times dropped from 4 hours to 5 minutes following paycheck distribution.

Recommendation: Arrive 90 minutes to 2 hours early for domestic flights today. Lines are moving efficiently at all terminals, though morning peaks (6–8:45 AM) showed longer waits. Terminal C may experience slightly higher volumes; other terminals are currently faster.
