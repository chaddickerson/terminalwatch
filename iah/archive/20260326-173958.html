Airport Update: IAH

TSA wait times at IAH remain significantly elevated today. Terminal E is experiencing extreme delays, with a reported 199-minute wait as of midday (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:54 PM), compared to a typical 36 minutes. Earlier reports indicated waits exceeding 3 hours at Terminal E, with lines stretching across multiple floors and outside the terminal (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:27 PM). Terminal A showed mixed results today: one traveler who arrived at 8:22 AM cleared security in under 45 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:38 PM), though another reported a 90-minute wait at 10:50 AM, despite the website showing 190 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 01:26 PM). The international-to-domestic TSA line reported under 10 minutes as of 1:32 PM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 01:32 PM).

System-wide, average wait times across all IAH checkpoints stood at 91 minutes as of 12:29 PM, roughly 68 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:29 PM). Some travelers reported longer journeys; one person waited approximately 2 hours in the regular security line (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 01:30 PM).

Arrive at least 4–5 hours before your flight, depending on whether you're checking bags. If checking luggage, plan for 5 hours; without bags, 4 hours should suffice (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 01:21 PM). Very early arrival (3:00–3:45 AM) has helped travelers avoid peak congestion.
