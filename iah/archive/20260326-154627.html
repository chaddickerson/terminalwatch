Airport Update: IAH

Today's TSA wait times at IAH remain severe across multiple terminals. Terminal E is experiencing particularly long delays, with a 3 hour 30 minute wait reported in standard lanes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:13 AM). Earlier today, Terminal E showed a 221-minute wait, 157 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:39 AM). One traveler reported a 3-hour wait after arriving at 6 AM and clearing security by 9 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:51 AM).

Terminal A is also significantly backed up. A 3-hour wait in standard lanes was reported (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:13 AM), and a traveler who arrived at 9:05 AM experienced a 50-minute wait at that time (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:12 AM).

Conflicting reports suggest CLEAR and PreCheck availability varies. One report states they have been shut down for days (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:41 AM), while another indicates they were open at Hobby (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:37 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 3+ hours early for flights from IAH today, particularly if departing during morning hours. Terminal E appears slowest; Terminal A also shows significant delays. Standard security lines are severely congested. Verify CLEAR and PreCheck status before arriving, as availability appears inconsistent.
