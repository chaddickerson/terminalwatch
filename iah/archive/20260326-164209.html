Terminal A: A traveler who arrived at 8:22 AM today cleared security in under 45 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:38 PM). The line was routed through the subway level but moved quickly, with ICE agents distributing water along the path.

Recommendation: Plan to arrive at least 3–4 hours early for domestic flights at IAH today.
