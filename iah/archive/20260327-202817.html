Terminal A is experiencing severe delays today. Wait times reached 165 minutes around 3:56 PM, far exceeding the typical 27-minute wait (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 3:56 PM). Earlier, Terminal A South reported 132 minutes at 12:55 PM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 12:55 PM).

Airport-wide, average wait times across all security lines reached 165 minutes at 3:06 PM, compared to a typical 22-minute average (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 3:06 PM). One traveler reported waiting 3 hours today (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 3:06 PM), though another managed to clear security in just 23 minutes by accessing an alternative line (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 3:09 PM).

Arrive at least 4–5 hours early for departing flights at both Terminal A and Terminal E. Staffing shortages are creating sustained bottlenecks. Ask TSA staff about alternative security lanes if available—some travelers have found shorter routes by inquiring directly.
