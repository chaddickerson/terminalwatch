Recommendation: Plan to arrive 2 hours before domestic departure at IAD. Wait times have been consistently short across all recent reports, with PreCheck and regular lines both moving efficiently. TSA PreCheck appears slightly faster when available, with several reports under 10 minutes, while regular lines have shown occasional morning congestion but nothing substantial. Current conditions suggest you could comfortably make your flight with standard arrival times.
