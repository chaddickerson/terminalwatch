Airport Update: IAD

TSA PreCheck lines at IAD have been moving exceptionally fast today. Touchless TSA Pre had barely a 1-minute wait (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 11:35 AM), and the main TSA PreCheck security line took only 17 minutes for a traveler who arrived at 6:10 AM (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 6:52 AM). Another traveler reported waiting 18 minutes earlier today (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 12:22 PM). Reports from yesterday also showed short waits: 7 minutes (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 11:30 PM) and 5–10 minutes in the afternoon (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 7:10 PM). One traveler using the touchless ID line found it nearly empty with 4–5 PreCheck lanes open (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 6:47 PM).

Recommendation: IAD security lines are running smoothly for both PreCheck and regular passengers. Arriving 90 minutes before departure should be sufficient today, though PreCheck holders may clear security in under 20 minutes. Regular security line wait times were not reported today, so allow extra buffer if you don't have PreCheck.
