TSA Watch: LGA

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 02:52 PM ET — 14 reports across 3 days

Mar 24: 2:34 PM

Terminal B

PreCheck lines at Terminal B showed significant variation today. During lunchtime, PreCheck wait times were approximately 30 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 1:24 PM). Earlier in the day at 12:05 PM, a traveler with PreCheck got through in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:59 PM). At 11:58 AM, the contrast between security lines was stark: the standard line wrapped across the entire terminal while PreCheck had only about 10 people deep (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 11:58 AM). A traveler also reported waiting 1 hour 37 minutes in the regular security line between 6:37 AM and 8:14 AM, though the line was moving surprisingly quickly despite crowds (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:00 PM).

General Reports (All Terminals)

Across LaGuardia today, average wait times have been significantly elevated. At 2:45 PM, TSA reported average wait times across all lines at 39 minutes, compared to a typical average of 7–8 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:45 PM). By midday at 12:03 PM, average wait times had peaked at 67 minutes, compared to a typical 8 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM). Earlier today at 10:03 AM, a traveler reported spending 2 hours in the TSA line (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:03 AM). Yesterday on Monday, one traveler spent 3 hours in the TSA line at LaGuardia (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 2:41 PM).

Recommendation

Plan to arrive significantly earlier than usual—today's average waits are running 30–60+ minutes longer than typical. If you have TSA PreCheck, you'll see meaningful time savings: today PreCheck lines ranged from 5 to 30 minutes, while regular lines wrapped across entire terminals and exceeded 1.5+ hours. Arrive at least 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights if you're in a regular security line; PreCheck holders can likely get away with 1.5 hours early, though conditions remain volatile.
