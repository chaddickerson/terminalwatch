Airport Update: LGA

Terminal B experienced severe congestion today. At 3:36 PM, a traveler reported 3 hours in the TSA line, with multiple passengers missing flights (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:36 PM). Earlier this morning at 9:15 AM, TSA PreCheck took approximately 15 minutes, while the regular security line wrapped around the terminal and outside the building (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 9:15 AM).

Terminal C reported 39 minutes average wait time at 3:08 PM today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:08 PM). Earlier at 6:06 AM, PreCheck lines were moving faster than CLEAR with PreCheck (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 6:06 AM).

Airport-wide, wait times peaked at 75 minutes average around midday (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:26 PM) before declining to 24 minutes by early evening (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:58 PM).

Arrive at least 2–3 hours early if flying from Terminal B without PreCheck; regular lines remain significantly backed up. PreCheck holders should plan 30–45 minutes. Terminal C conditions are comparatively better but still elevated.
