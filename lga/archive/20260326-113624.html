Terminal C

PreCheck lines moved significantly faster than CLEAR this morning. A traveler with TSA PreCheck Touchless cleared security in 29 minutes at 5:44 AM today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:44 AM). Another PreCheck passenger reported clearing in 30 minutes at 5:50 AM today, noting that regular PreCheck and TSA Touchless lines were faster than CLEAR with PreCheck (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:12 AM). Earlier on Wednesday evening, a digital ID line took approximately 3 minutes at 5:30 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 7:40 PM).

Terminal B

Conditions were extremely congested this morning. The TSA PreCheck line alone was heavily backed up at 5:30 AM today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:43 AM). At 4:30 AM today, the security line snaked through the terminal four times (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:32 AM). On Wednesday evening, a traveler cleared security in less than 10 minutes at approximately 7:56 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:41 PM).

General Reports (All Terminals)

Overall wait times today have been severe. TSA reported an average wait of 58 minutes across all lines at 7:19 AM today, compared to a typical 10 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:19 AM). Staff were indicating at least 3 hour waits for newly arriving passengers at 6:49 AM today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:49 AM). One traveler betting on PreCheck wait times suggested they could exceed 2.5 hours at 7:06 AM today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:06 AM). On Wednesday, a traveler with PreCheck reported a 15–20 minute wait while the regular line exceeded 3 hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:18 PM). A passenger without PreCheck waited 2 hours 30 minutes on Wednesday (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 2:38 PM).

Recommendation

Today's conditions at LGA have been exceptionally heavy, with regular security lines exceeding 3 hours. If you have TSA PreCheck, plan to arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights, though Terminal C PreCheck passengers have moved through in around 30 minutes; consider this when choosing your terminal if possible. Without PreCheck, plan at least 3–4 hours early. If you have access to TSA PreCheck Touchless or digital ID in Terminal C, those lines appear to be moving faster than standard CLEAR lines.
