Airport Update: LGA

Terminal B: Wait times today have been severe. A traveler reported 3 hours in the regular security line earlier today, with Southwest agents noting this has been typical for over a week (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:36 PM). Earlier this morning, TSA PreCheck took 15 minutes, while regular lines wrapped around the terminal and outside the building (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 9:15 AM).

Terminal C: Wait times reached 39 minutes today, 32 minutes above typical (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:08 PM). Early morning reports noted PreCheck moved faster than CLEAR with PreCheck (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 6:06 AM).

Across LGA: Average wait times today peaked at 75 minutes around midday (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:26 PM), then declined to 24 minutes by late afternoon (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:58 PM). One traveler waited 1 hour in the regular line (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:01 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 2.5–3 hours early if you don't have TSA PreCheck, especially at Terminal B. PreCheck holders should plan 30–45 minutes for security. CLEAR may not guarantee speed today based on reports.
