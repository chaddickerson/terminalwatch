TSA Watch: LGA

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 03:23 PM ET — 15 reports across 3 days

Mar 24: 3:21 PM|2:34 PM

PreCheck was approximately 30 minutes during lunchtime today at Terminal B (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 1:24 PM). Earlier today at Terminal B, the standard security line wrapped across the entire terminal while the PreCheck line was only about 10 people deep (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 11:58 AM). This morning at 6:00 AM, a traveler reported PreCheck was 30 minutes while the regular line was 1 hour; another traveler heard from someone who waited 4 hours (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:13 PM).

Across all terminals, wait times have been significantly elevated throughout the day. The most recent airport-wide report showed an average wait of 22 minutes (compared to a typical 6–7 minutes) (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:10 PM). Earlier this afternoon, averages peaked at 46 minutes, more than 40 minutes above normal (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:02 PM). However, one traveler with PreCheck who arrived at 12:05 PM cleared security in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:59 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 1.5 to 2 hours early if you're using a regular security line today—conditions have been unusually congested throughout the afternoon. If you have TSA PreCheck, you can plan for a shorter window; today's reports show PreCheck moving significantly faster than standard lines, though even PreCheck has experienced 30-minute waits during peak times. Arriving early remains the safest strategy given the elevated demand.
