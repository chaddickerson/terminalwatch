Terminal B: The TSA line in Terminal B was backed up significantly today, with reports that it extended to the parking garage (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:36 PM).

General reports across LaGuardia: Wait times today were substantially elevated. As of this evening, average wait times across all lines were 46 minutes, compared to a typical average of 6 minutes—a difference of +40 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:02 PM). Earlier today at 3:59 PM, the average was 34 minutes, a +28 minute increase over typical conditions (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:59 PM). Multiple travelers reported extreme delays: one waited 2 hours 39 minutes to get through security today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:40 PM), and another spent 2 hours 30 minutes in the regular (non-PreCheck) line and noted few ICE agents visible despite staffing challenges (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 2:38 PM). The TSA PreCheck line was also heavily congested today, snaking back three times over (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:10 PM). A traveler missed their flight after spending 3 hours in the TSA line today (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 1:00 PM). One report noted that the Delta Sky Club line was shorter than the TSA line this afternoon (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:44 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive significantly earlier than usual—at least 3 to 3.5 hours before domestic flights today. Both PreCheck and regular lines are experiencing substantial delays. While one traveler reported zero wait using ICE (Improvised Credential Exchange) yesterday evening, current data does not indicate PreCheck or expedited options are providing meaningful time savings today.
