Airport Update: LGA

Terminal B experienced severe delays throughout the morning. PreCheck wait times ranged from 1 hour 27 minutes at 6:30 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:31 AM) to at least 2 hours later (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:12 AM). Regular security lines were significantly worse, with one traveler reporting staff estimated 2–3 hours wait times (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 7:07 AM). An earlier report noted 2.5 hours combined for bag drop and security (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 5:19 AM).

Terminal C showed much faster movement. The TSA touchless line at Delta took only 7 minutes at 8:52 AM, with the regular line also appearing short (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:04 AM).

General TSA wait times across all LGA lines remained elevated throughout the day. By early afternoon, wait times were 29 minutes (typical: 10 minutes) at 1:30 PM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 1:30 PM) and 28 minutes (typical: 11 minutes) at 12:40 PM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 12:40 PM). A separate PreCheck report noted 30 minutes at 10 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 10:29 AM), while another traveler reported 43 minutes in the PreCheck line earlier in the morning (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 10:20 AM).

Arrive 3–4 hours early if flying from Terminal B without PreCheck; Terminal C travelers may manage with 2–2.5 hours. PreCheck holders should still plan an extra hour beyond typical arrival time today.
