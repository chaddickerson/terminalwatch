Airport Update: LGA

TSA wait times at LaGuardia today are significantly longer than normal. Current average wait times across all lines are 75 minutes, compared to a typical 9 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:26 PM). Individual reports show wide variation: one traveler waited 2.5 hours in a regular line (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:24 AM), while another reported 65 minutes at 10 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:06 AM), and a third got through in just 1 hour (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:01 AM).

Terminal B shows the starkest disparity between security lines. TSA PreCheck was moving quickly at 15–20 minutes this morning (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 09:15 AM), while regular security lines wrapped around the terminal and outside the building, with at least one traveler missing her flight after a 3+ hour wait (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:11 AM). Terminal C PreCheck lines showed surprising speed this morning (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 06:06 AM).

Recommendation: If you don't have TSA PreCheck, plan to arrive 3+ hours early for domestic flights today. PreCheck holders should plan for 20–30 minutes. Terminal B regular lines are particularly backed up; PreCheck/CLEAR members will experience dramatically faster processing.
