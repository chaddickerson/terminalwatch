Airport Update: LGA

Terminal B is experiencing severe delays today. One traveler reported a 3-hour wait earlier this afternoon, with multiple passengers missing flights—a Southwest agent indicated this has been the norm for over a week (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 03:36 PM). This morning, TSA PreCheck moved relatively quickly at 15 minutes, but the regular security line wrapped around the terminal and extended outside the building (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 09:15 AM).

Terminal C reported a 39-minute wait at 3:08 PM, significantly above the typical 7-minute average (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 03:08 PM). Early this morning, regular PreCheck lines moved surprisingly fast, though CLEAR with PreCheck was notably slower (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 06:06 AM).

Airport-wide, wait times peaked at 75 minutes around midday (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:26 PM), then ranged from 22 to 65 minutes through the afternoon. Individual travelers reported 1 to 2.5 hours in security lines this morning (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:13 AM, Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:24 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive at least 3 hours early for domestic flights, especially at Terminal B where waits have been extreme. TSA PreCheck offers a significant advantage—waits are typically 15–30 minutes versus 60+ minutes for regular lines. CLEAR may not provide its usual speed advantage currently.
