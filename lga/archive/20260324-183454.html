TSA Watch: LGA

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 02:01 PM ET — 11 reports across 3 days

Mar 24: 1:59 PM|1:34 PM|12:37 PM|11:40 AM|11:17 AM|10:40 AM|9:57 AM|9:24 AM

Terminal B at LGA is experiencing highly variable wait times. Most recent reports from Tuesday afternoon (Tue Mar 24, 12:59 PM) show 5 minutes for TSA PreCheck, while the standard security line wrapped across the entire terminal (Tue Mar 24, 11:58 AM). However, midday reports also noted around 30 minutes for PreCheck (Tue Mar 24, 1:24 PM), suggesting inconsistent conditions even within the same day.

Earlier in the week, conditions were significantly worse. An early morning traveler (Tue Mar 24, 11:44 AM) waited 1 hour 37 minutes in the standard line (arriving 6:37 AM, clearing by 8:14 AM), and another report documented a 2-hour wait (Tue Mar 24, 10:03 AM). Over the weekend, waits peaked at 2–3 hours for both standard and PreCheck lines (Sun Mar 22, 4:37 AM and Sun Mar 22, 6:20 PM). Monday saw reports of 3-hour waits (Mon Mar 23, 2:41 PM).

PreCheck clearly makes a difference when lines are heavy. At peak times (Tue Mar 24, 11:58 AM), standard security wrapped the terminal while PreCheck was only 10 people deep. Outside rush periods, PreCheck moved travelers through in 20 minutes (Sun Mar 22, 1:51 PM).

Arrive at least 2 hours early for morning flights and 90 minutes for afternoon departures. If you don't have PreCheck, add extra buffer time—waits for standard security have ranged from 1.5 to 3 hours over the past few days. Early morning (before 6 AM) and mid-afternoon periods show the most unpredictable conditions.
