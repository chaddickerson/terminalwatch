Airport Update: LGA

Today at LaGuardia, TSA PreCheck continues to offer dramatically faster service. A traveler cleared the PreCheck line in just 10 minutes this morning, even as regular security lines ran significantly longer (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 8:20 AM). Another passenger reported the fastest security experience they'd ever had at LGA today, though the checkpoint area was notably staffed with ICE agents (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 10:58 AM).

Early this morning, TSA reported average wait times across all lines at 22 minutes—significantly above the typical 4-minute average—indicating elevated congestion today (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 3:06 AM). A traveler noted that while regular security lines stretched for hours, PreCheck passengers moved through in minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 9:47 AM).

Recommendation: If you have TSA PreCheck, plan to arrive 15–20 minutes before your security screening. Without PreCheck, allow an extra 30–45 minutes due to today's extended regular line waits. PreCheck is significantly faster at LGA currently.
