Terminal B

PreCheck lines at Terminal B were 15–20 minutes around midday today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:50 AM). However, earlier this morning between 5–8 AM, Terminal B experienced severe congestion, with wait times stretching to approximately 3 hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:10 AM).

Terminal C

A traveler with PreCheck and CLEAR reported passing through Terminal C in just 5 minutes earlier today, noting that PreCheck and CLEAR made minimal difference since the line was so short overall (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:35 PM).

General reports (all terminals)

Conditions at LGA have been highly variable today. A traveler with Touchless (same line as PreCheck) reported clearing security in ~2 minutes at noon, describing all lines as "very, very short" and the airport as "oddly empty" (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:12 PM). However, other reports the same hour indicated wait times of approximately 3 hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:04 PM) and an estimated 3 hours with lines wrapping around the terminal twice (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 10:43 AM).

Earlier today, the TSA sensor reported an average wait of 77 minutes across all lines—a 71-minute increase over the typical 6-minute average (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:57 AM). A boots-on-the-ground report at 9:57 AM confirmed lines were approximately 70 minutes but noted they were moving steadily (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:57 AM). Another traveler reported waiting ~72 minutes earlier in the morning (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:01 AM).

Recommendation

Conditions at LGA today are inconsistent and unpredictable, ranging from minimal waits to 3 hours. Plan to arrive at least 2–3 hours before your flight. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, prioritize Terminal C, which showed notably faster processing today; Terminal B had 15–20 minute waits at midday but severe delays earlier in the morning. Be aware that even with PreCheck, you may face significant delays depending on the time and terminal.
