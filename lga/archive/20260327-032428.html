Airport Update: LGA

Terminal B saw severe congestion today. A traveler reported 3 hours in the regular security line earlier this afternoon, with many passengers missing flights (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:36 PM). This morning, TSA PreCheck took 15 minutes, while the regular line wrapped around the terminal and outside the building (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 9:15 AM). A separate report noted 1 hour in PreCheck during the early morning (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 12:36 PM).

Terminal C reported 39 minutes wait time, about 32 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:08 PM).

Across LaGuardia overall, wait times spiked dramatically today. Peak delays reached 65 minutes (versus typical 7–8 minutes) around mid-afternoon (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 2:22 PM). By late afternoon, average waits had moderated to 24 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:58 PM).

Arrive at least 2–3 hours early, especially if you don't have TSA PreCheck. Terminal B is particularly congested; PreCheck offers significantly faster access but still expect 15+ minutes. CLEAR members reported smoother screening.
