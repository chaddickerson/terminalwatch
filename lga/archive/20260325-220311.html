Terminal B

The TSA PreCheck line at Terminal B was severely backed up today, with both touchless and regular PreCheck lines merged into a single queue as of 1:15 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:31 PM). At that time, touchless PreCheck and CLEAR were both down (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:24 PM), leaving only the merged PreCheck line and the regular standard line as options. By 2:36 PM, the regular security line in Terminal B had backed up to the parking garage (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:36 PM).

General Reports (All Terminals)

Wait times across LaGuardia deteriorated significantly throughout today. The most recent official report, at 6:02 PM, showed average wait times of 46 minutes across all lines—a spike of 40 minutes above the typical 6-minute average (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:02 PM). Earlier in the afternoon at 3:59 PM, wait times were 34 minutes, up 28 minutes from typical (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:59 PM). At 1:58 PM, the average had been 72 minutes, exceeding normal waits by 64 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:58 PM).

Individual travelers reported extreme waits today. One passenger waited 2 hours and 39 minutes to clear security (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:40 PM), while another in the regular (non-PreCheck) line spent 2 hours and 30 minutes waiting and noted that staff distributed water bottles and lollipops (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 2:38 PM). Another traveler reported a 3-hour wait (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:58 PM), and one passenger missed their flight after waiting in line for over 3 hours (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 1:00 PM).

ICE agents were present in the terminal today undergoing training on scanner and check-in procedures (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:51 PM). One traveler noted that the Sky Club line was longer than the TSA line at one point in the afternoon (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:44 PM).

Recommendation

Plan to arrive at least 3–4 hours early for flights from LaGuardia today. Regular security lines are moving significantly slower than PreCheck lines, though PreCheck itself has faced substantial delays and technical issues (touchless PreCheck was down at Terminal B). If you have PreCheck or CLEAR access, use it, though be aware that both services experienced disruptions today. Current wait times are running well above normal across all terminals.
