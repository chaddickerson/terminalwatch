A traveler reported zero wait at LaGuardia's TSA checkpoint today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:13 PM). However, another traveler today described the security line looping around the terminal four times (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:47 PM). A third report from earlier today indicated a regular security line wait of 29 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:46 PM).

On Sunday, Mar 22, a traveler with TSA PreCheck at the American Airlines terminal waited just 20 minutes from Uber through security (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 1:51 PM). That same day, another traveler reported spending 2 hours in the regular security line, which wrapped around the entire terminal, and noted that the TSA PreCheck line was similarly congested (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 6:20 PM). An early morning departure on Sunday also encountered a 2–3 hour line, with the PreCheck line also described as "psychotic" (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 4:37 AM).

On Monday, Mar 23, a traveler spent 3 hours waiting in the TSA line at LaGuardia (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 2:41 PM).

Recommendation: Wait times at LaGuardia vary significantly. If traveling today, plan to arrive at least 2–3 hours early for domestic flights, as reports range from zero minutes to multiple hours of delays. TSA PreCheck does not appear to offer consistent relief; one recent report showed a 20-minute PreCheck wait, but another indicated PreCheck lines were equally congested. CLEAR status was not mentioned in available reports. Arriving very early in the morning (before 6 AM) or during off-peak hours may offer better throughput.
