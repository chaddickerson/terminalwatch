Terminal C

Regular PreCheck lines were moving notably faster than CLEAR lines today. A traveler who went through regular PreCheck took approximately 30 minutes at 5:50 AM, noting that the lines extended out to the parking garages but that regular PreCheck was surprisingly fast (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:12 AM). Another traveler confirmed this pattern, reporting that the TSA PreCheck Touchless line moved significantly faster than the CLEAR line, getting through in 29 minutes at 5:43 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:44 AM). A digital ID line was reported to have taken only approximately 3 minutes just after 5:30 PM on March 26 (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 7:40 PM).

Terminal B

Conditions were chaotic early this morning. At 5:30 AM, the TSA PreCheck line alone was notably long, with a photo showing significant congestion (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:43 AM). Earlier, at 4:30 AM, the line snaked four times through the terminal (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:32 AM). On the evening of March 25, a traveler got through the domestic security checkpoint in less than 10 minutes starting around 7:56 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:41 PM).

General Reports Across LGA

Airport-wide wait times have been significantly elevated today. As of 6:24 AM today, average wait times across all lines were 58 minutes, compared to a typical 10 minutes—a difference of +48 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:24 AM). Six minutes earlier, the average was 58 minutes against a typical 8 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:01 AM). At 4:00 AM today, wait times were 50 minutes versus a typical 10 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:00 AM).

On March 25, PreCheck lines were approximately 15–20 minutes, while regular lines reached 3+ hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:18 PM). Evening wait times on March 25 peaked at 83 minutes average across all lines (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:04 PM). A regular (non-PreCheck) traveler reported spending 2 hours 30 minutes in line on March 25 afternoon (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 2:38 PM).

Recommendations

If traveling through LGA today, plan to arrive significantly earlier than normal. TSA PreCheck and digital ID lines are your fastest option—regular PreCheck at Terminal C has been running 30 minutes this morning, while regular (non-PreCheck) lines are experiencing multi-hour delays. Avoid CLEAR at Terminal C if you have PreCheck, as the regular PreCheck Touchless line is substantially faster. If you don't have PreCheck, expect waits of 2–3+ hours and arrive as early as possible.
