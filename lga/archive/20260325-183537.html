At Terminal B, conditions have been highly variable today. The PreCheck line experienced severe congestion earlier this afternoon, with reports of it snaking up and back 3 times (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:10 PM). At 1:15 PM, both Touchless TSA PreCheck and CLEAR were down, forcing all PreCheck users into a single merged line, while regular security remained separate (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:24 PM). However, earlier in the morning around 11:50 AM, PreCheck moved much faster at 15–20 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:50 AM).

At Terminal C, wait times have been severe. One traveler reported approximately four hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:16 PM), and another missed their flight after waiting 3 hours and 1 minute (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 1:00 PM). One bright exception: a traveler with PreCheck/CLEAR got through in just 5 minutes at 12:35 PM, noting that Terminal C is primarily served by Delta and experienced minimal congestion at that time, though they noted PreCheck/CLEAR made little difference then (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:35 PM).

Reports not tied to a specific terminal indicate severe airport-wide congestion today. Overall TSA wait times across all lines averaged 72 minutes by early afternoon—64 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:58 PM). An unattributed report stated the general TSA line at LaGuardia was 3 hours long (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:01 PM), and another traveler was told to expect a 3-hour wait (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:04 PM). By late morning, waits were estimated at 3 hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:19 PM). A notable exception occurred around noon, when one traveler with Touchless PreCheck got through in approximately 2 minutes, finding all lines very short (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:12 PM). The TSA line was also reported to wrap around the terminal twice (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 10:43 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive at LGA at least 3–4 hours early for a domestic flight today. Wait times have ranged dramatically from near-zero to over 4 hours, with most reports clustering around 2–3 hours. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, note that these have provided inconsistent advantages today—in some cases they cut wait times significantly, but at Terminal B both services were temporarily down, and at Terminal C one traveler found they made minimal difference during peak periods. Terminal C may be slightly faster during off-peak windows, but all terminals are experiencing severe congestion. Arrive as early as possible and avoid peak hours (10 AM–2 PM) if your flight permits flexibility.
