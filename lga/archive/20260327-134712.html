Airport Update: LGA

Today at LaGuardia, TSA wait times are significantly longer than typical. As of 9:44 AM, average wait times across all lines stood at 23 minutes, compared to a typical 8 minutes—a difference of 15 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 09:44 AM).

Terminal B: Conditions have been severe this morning. A traveler reported the PreCheck line took 1 hour and 27 minutes at 6:30 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 08:31 AM). Another reported at least 2 hours in the PreCheck line this morning (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 08:12 AM). A traveler arriving 4+ hours before departure reported staff estimated 2–3 hours without PreCheck (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 07:07 AM).

Terminal C: A touchless PreCheck line took only 7 minutes at 8:52 AM, with the regular line also appearing short (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 09:04 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive 3–4 hours early if flying from Terminal B without PreCheck; conditions there are exceptionally congested. Terminal C appears faster. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, allow at least 1–2 hours in Terminal B, though conditions may improve as the morning progresses.
