Recommendation: Plan to arrive at least 2.5 to 3 hours early for flights today. Terminal C is your best option if available, with significantly shorter wait times than Terminal B. Standard security lines are experiencing 70+ minute delays throughout the day. If you have TSA PreCheck, expect 30–40 minute waits—still substantially above normal. Avoid Terminal B if possible during peak hours.
