Terminal B: Conditions today have been severe. A traveler reported that TSA PreCheck was approximately 38 minutes at around 4:30 AM, with the line appearing roughly 10 minutes longer by 5 AM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 05:21 AM). By mid-morning, Terminal B was experiencing extreme congestion, with one traveler advising others to avoid the terminal entirely between 5–8 AM, noting that waits could exceed 3 hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 08:10 AM).

Terminal C: A traveler cleared TSA in just 10 minutes using the touchless ID terminal at 6:31 AM today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 06:31 AM).

Across all terminals today, average wait times have been significantly elevated. At 7:57 AM, airport-wide TSA wait times were reported at 71 minutes—more than six times the typical 10-minute average (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 07:57 AM). Earlier, at 5:57 AM, airport-wide waits averaged 50 minutes, still 40 minutes above normal (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 05:57 AM). Individual travelers reported waits of approximately 1 hour 10 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 07:17 AM) and 1.5–2 hours (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 07:09 AM) in regular security lines. A recheck line processed travelers in 30 seconds (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 08:06 AM).

For general (non-terminal-specific) reports: Yesterday evening, a traveler without PreCheck or CLEAR reported a 29-minute wait (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 09:46 PM). On Sunday, conditions were also challenging, with one traveler spending 2 hours in a regular line that wrapped around the entire terminal before reaching the traditional security checkpoint, and another reporting that TSA PreCheck was not significantly faster (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 06:20 PM). However, another traveler at the American Airlines terminal completed TSA PreCheck in 20 minutes from arrival (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 01:51 PM). On Monday, a traveler reported spending 3 hours in line (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 02:41 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive at LGA substantially earlier than usual today. Terminal B is experiencing the worst delays and should be avoided if possible during peak morning hours; if you must use Terminal B, allow at least 3 hours before your flight. Terminal C appears to offer faster processing, particularly for those using touchless ID. If you have TSA PreCheck, expect waits of 30–40 minutes even in dedicated PreCheck lanes. Regular security lines are averaging 70+ minutes across the airport. Arriving 3–4 hours early is strongly advised for all terminals and security types today.
