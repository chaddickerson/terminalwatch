A traveler reported a zero wait at LaGuardia's TSA checkpoint (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:13 PM). Another traveler whose friend visited LaGuardia this morning reported a 29-minute wait in the regular security line (no PreCheck or CLEAR) (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:46 PM).

Earlier reports from the weekend showed significantly longer waits. A traveler spent two hours getting through security at LaGuardia, with lines wrapping around the entire terminal before reaching the traditional security line start; the TSA PreCheck line was similarly congested (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 6:20 PM). However, another traveler using TSA PreCheck at the American Airlines terminal reported only a 20-minute wait from Uber through security (Bluesky - Sun Mar 22, 1:51 PM). A separate traveler spent 3 hours in the TSA queue at LaGuardia on an earlier date (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 2:41 PM).

Recommendation: Recent reports suggest conditions have improved considerably, with some travelers reporting minimal or no waits. Arriving 1.5 to 2 hours before domestic departure should be sufficient for most travelers. If you have TSA PreCheck, you may be able to arrive closer to 1 hour before departure, though weekend and early-morning travel may warrant additional buffer time based on recent variability.
