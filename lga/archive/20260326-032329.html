General Reports
PreCheck lines were moving significantly faster than regular lines today. PreCheck wait times were approximately 15–20 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:18 PM), while regular security lines experienced substantially longer delays, with one traveler reporting a wait of 2 hours 30 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 2:38 PM).

Average wait times across all TSA lines at LGA showed elevated delays throughout the evening. At 8:04 PM, average wait times reached 83 minutes compared to a typical 3 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:04 PM). Earlier in the day, at 6:02 PM, average waits were 46 minutes versus a typical 6 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:02 PM), and at 3:59 PM they were 34 minutes above normal (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:59 PM).

Recommendation
If you have PreCheck or a digital ID option, use it—waits in those dedicated lines today were under 20 minutes. For regular security lines, plan to arrive at least 2–3 hours early based on today's evening delays. Terminal B and Terminal C showed faster processing when used, so if you have flexibility on which terminal to use, either of those may be preferable. Conditions improved somewhat earlier in the afternoon, so morning or early-afternoon departure times may experience shorter waits than evening departures.
