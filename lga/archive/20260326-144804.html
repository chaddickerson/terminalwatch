Airport Update: LGA

Terminal B is experiencing severe delays today. TSA PreCheck is moving relatively quickly at 15–20 minutes (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 09:15 AM, Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:11 AM), but regular security lines are wrapped around the terminal and extending outside the building, with at least one traveler reporting a 3+ hour wait that caused them to miss their flight (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:11 AM). Another traveler confirmed a 3-hour wait in the standard line (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:06 AM).

Terminal C saw much faster service, with one traveler clearing security in just 5 minutes at 8:00 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 08:09 AM). Regular PreCheck lines were reportedly the fastest option at Terminal C (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 06:06 AM).

Terminal A had minimal wait times this morning (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 07:53 AM).

Overall TSA wait times at LaGuardia averaged 43 minutes across all lines today, significantly above the typical 6-minute average (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 08:26 AM).

Recommendation: If flying from Terminal B without TSA PreCheck today, plan for 3+ hours before your flight. PreCheck holders should arrive 30–45 minutes early. Terminal C appears significantly faster and may be a better option if available.
