Airport Update: LGA

TSA PreCheck continues to offer significant time savings at LaGuardia today. Recommendation: Plan to arrive 90 minutes before departure for domestic flights if using a regular security line today. PreCheck lines are moving considerably faster than regular lanes, making enrollment worthwhile for frequent travelers.
