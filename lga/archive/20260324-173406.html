Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 12:39 PM ET — 12 reports across 3 days

Earlier reports: Mar 24, 2026 12:37 PM ET|Mar 24, 2026 11:40 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 11:17 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 10:40 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 09:57 AM ET|Mar 24, 2026 09:24 AM ET

Current conditions (Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM ET): Average wait times across all TSA lines at LGA are 67 minutes—nearly 8 times the typical 8-minute average. Terminal B has been hit particularly hard, with the standard security line wrapping across the entire terminal (Tue Mar 24, 11:58 AM ET).

PreCheck vs. standard lines: TSA PreCheck is significantly faster but not immune to delays. At Terminal B, PreCheck had roughly 10 people waiting (Tue Mar 24, 11:58 AM ET), compared to the main line's extensive wrap-around. However, one traveler reported PreCheck lines were equally backed up on Sunday (Sun Mar 22, 4:37 AM ET). By contrast, an American Airlines Terminal traveler cleared PreCheck in just 20 minutes (Sun Mar 22, 1:51 PM ET).

Time-of-day patterns: Early morning departures face extreme congestion. One traveler waited 1 hour 37 minutes from 6:37 AM to 8:14 AM at Terminal B (Tue Mar 24, 11:44 AM ET), while another reported 2–3 hour waits for a 6 AM flight (Sun Mar 22, 4:37 AM ET). Sunday afternoon saw 2-hour waits as well (Sun Mar 22, 06:20 PM ET), suggesting sustained heavy traffic across multiple times of day.

Recommendation: Arrive at least 3 hours early for domestic flights, even with PreCheck. Current conditions far exceed normal patterns, and early morning flights are especially vulnerable to missing departure times if you underestimate the queue.
