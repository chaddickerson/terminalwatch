Recommendation

If flying from Terminal B, plan to arrive at least 1.5 to 2 hours early for domestic flights. TSA PreCheck offers a meaningful advantage—today's data shows PreCheck lines roughly 30 minutes shorter than standard lines—so enrollment is worthwhile if you fly frequently. For early morning departures (before 7:00 AM), allow extra time regardless of PreCheck status, as weekend and early-week congestion patterns show unpredictable delays.
