Airport Update: LAX

LAX security checkpoints are moving very quickly today. One traveler reported clearing TSA in just 3 minutes earlier this morning (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 07:15 AM), and another described the airport as "quiet as a church mouse" with no traffic outside (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 07:15 AM).

Terminal 7 continues to show excellent throughput. TSA PreCheck lines have been nearly instant, with one traveler reporting zero wait at the ID check and only 1–2 minutes at bag check early this morning (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 09:47 AM). Another PreCheck traveler passed through in 5 minutes flat, with regular security appearing trivial as well (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 11:30 AM).

Terminal 3 showed 30-minute wait times earlier in the week (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 12:57 PM), though current conditions appear much lighter.

Arrival recommendation: Plan to arrive 2 hours early for domestic flights. PreCheck and CLEAR members should experience minimal delays—often under 5 minutes—so 90 minutes may suffice if you hold PreCheck.
