Recommendation: Plan to arrive 30–40 minutes before your flight for regular TSA, or 20–25 minutes if you have PreCheck. Today's shorter waits reflect typical Tuesday traffic; expect longer lines on weekends and Fridays. PreCheck and Touchless PreCheck substantially cut wait times and are worth considering for frequent travelers.
