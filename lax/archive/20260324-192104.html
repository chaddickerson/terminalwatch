Arrival recommendation: Plan to arrive 30–40 minutes before your flight if you have TSA PreCheck, and 45–60 minutes early for regular screening. Today's wait times suggest shorter queues overall, with PreCheck significantly faster than standard lines. Early morning flights (before 8 a.m.) appear to have the shortest waits.
