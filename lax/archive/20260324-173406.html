Clear patterns emerged by time of day: Early morning departures (before 5 AM) consistently showed the shortest waits at 10–20 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 5:47 AM ET through 08:10 AM ET), while late evening (Mon Mar 23, 11:09 PM ET) also showed quick processing. Mid-morning (9–10 AM) and afternoon hours tended toward normal to moderate delays.

Recommendation: Arrive 90 minutes early for standard security, or 60 minutes early if you have TSA PreCheck. If flying an early morning departure (before 6 AM), you can plan for shorter waits and arrive 75 minutes before departure. Consider investing in PreCheck if you travel LAX regularly—the difference between 2–5 minutes and 20–30 minutes is substantial during peak periods.
