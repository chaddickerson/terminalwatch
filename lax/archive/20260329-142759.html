Airport Update: LAX

Today at LAX, security screening moved exceptionally quickly. TSA processed travelers in less than 5 minutes early this morning (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 8:54 AM), and another traveler reported a 2-minute wait earlier in the week (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 1:46 AM).

Arrive 1–2 hours early for domestic flights—well below the standard 2–3 hours—though PreCheck members can plan even more conservatively given current conditions.
