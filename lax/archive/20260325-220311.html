A general report mentioned 10 minutes to get through (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:24 PM).

Recommendation
Based on today's reports, LAX security lines are moving exceptionally fast. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, arriving 90 minutes before domestic departure should be more than sufficient, especially at Terminals 7 and Bradley. Regular security travelers should plan for 2 hours early arrival, though you may find yourself through checkpoint in 15–30 minutes. Terminal 3 showed slightly longer waits (30 minutes) this morning, so consider adding an extra buffer there if that's your terminal.
