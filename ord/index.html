On Monday, TSA wait times were reported at an average of 83 minutes across all lines, significantly higher than the typical 3–4 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 2:16 PM). TSA PreCheck at O'Hare on Sunday took 5 minutes total for one traveler (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 12:56 PM), and another TSA PreCheck user cleared Terminal 5 in 5 minutes on Sunday morning (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 1:19 PM).

Recommendation: Today's reports show dramatically improved conditions compared to earlier in the week. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, arrive 15–20 minutes before your domestic flight, based on today's near-zero waits in Terminal 1. For regular screening, plan 30–45 minutes before departure, though conditions appear much more normal than Monday's extended delays. Terminal 1 and Terminal 5 are showing particularly fast processing today.
