General Reports (Non-Terminal-Specific)

Today, reports show highly variable conditions at O'Hare. One traveler arrived 4 hours early due to concerns about long lines but found the airport to be a ghost town at the TSA checkpoint (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 4:21 PM). However, another report from earlier today indicated a traveler waited 5.5 hours to get through TSA at O'Hare this morning (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:12 PM). A third traveler reported 36 minutes total from parking lot to gate, noting that TSA PreCheck lines were problematic but regular screening was typical (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 1:58 PM). This morning, one traveler cleared security in 20 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:27 AM), and another got through in 20 minutes (entering at 9:29 AM and clearing by 9:49 AM) (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:37 AM).

Yesterday, a travel alert reported average TSA wait times across all lines at 83 minutes, significantly longer than the typical 3–4 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 2:16 PM and Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 2:09 PM). However, other reports from yesterday showed much faster times, with one TSA PreCheck traveler waiting only 30 minutes across recent trips to O'Hare, Philadelphia, and Boston (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 10:57 AM) and another clearing security in 20 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 10:48 AM). Earlier last week, one traveler reported 15 minutes through TSA with PreCheck on a Sunday (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 11:21 PM), and another reported 15 minutes the previous day (Bluesky - Sat Mar 21, 8:08 PM). General observations yesterday noted that TSA lines were basically non-existent with no ICE in sight (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 8:35 AM).

Recommendations

Today's reports are contradictory, with waits ranging from near-zero to over 5 hours. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR—especially traveling from Terminal 5—you may be able to get through in 5–10 minutes. For regular security lines today, conditions are unpredictable; plan for 20–40 minutes as a conservative baseline, but arrive 2–3 hours early for domestic flights and 3 hours early for international flights to account for today's volatility. If you don't have PreCheck or CLEAR, consider that regular lines appear slower than PreCheck lanes based on available reports, so add extra buffer time accordingly.
