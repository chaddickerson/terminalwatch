At ORD generally, wait times have been reasonable this week. A traveler got through today in about 15 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:03 AM). TSA PreCheck has been notably faster, with one traveler clearing security in under 5 minutes last weekend (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 1:41 PM). Overall, a traveler noted that wait times this past week have been "totally reasonable and not out of the ordinary" compared to other cities (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 11:56 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 90 minutes before departure for domestic flights. TSA PreCheck and CLEAR users can arrive closer to 60 minutes before departure, as PreCheck lines have consistently been among the shortest at ORD. If you're using regular security, allow extra time on weekday mornings, though current reports suggest 15–20 minutes is typical.
