Recommendation

Plan to arrive 1.5 to 2 hours before departure. Terminal 5 data shows notably faster processing, so if you're flying out of that terminal, you may be able to cut this shorter. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, expect significantly faster times—some users are clearing in under 10 minutes—though at least one recent report suggests PreCheck lines can occasionally lag behind regular lines. Without PreCheck or CLEAR, budget closer to the full 1.5–2 hours, especially during peak times.
