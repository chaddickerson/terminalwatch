Airport Update: ATL

This evening, security lines at ATL were moving exceptionally fast. One traveler cleared security in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Sat Apr 04, 6:22 PM), while PreCheck passengers reported a 10-minute wait (Twitter - Sat Apr 04, 9:22 AM).

Earlier today, conditions improved significantly from the morning. By mid-morning, the Main, North, and Lower North checkpoints were all under 10 minutes, and the South PreCheck-only line had no wait (Bluesky - Sat Apr 04, 7:54 AM). However, wait times had been longer during the early morning hours, with reports of up to 40 minutes (Twitter - Sat Apr 04, 3:51 AM).

Recommendation: Today's data shows exceptional throughput, especially in evening hours and for PreCheck holders. Aim to arrive 15–20 minutes before your flight for standard screening, or 10–15 minutes if using PreCheck. If flying in the morning, allow extra time and consider PreCheck for faster processing.
