Airport Update: ATL

Early this morning at ATL, PreCheck and expedited services were largely unavailable. A traveler who arrived at 5:55 AM reported the PreCheck line extended all the way back to Carousel 2, with TSA agents moving people through efficiently; that traveler cleared security by 6:35 AM (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 6:41 AM). Around 4 AM, the general line stretched down the hallway to Applebees, while PreCheck had approximately an hour wait and CLEAR was not being accepted (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 4:58 AM). Earlier overnight, a traveler reported clearing security in just 40 minutes at 2 AM (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 5:54 AM), and another got through in 15 minutes at the same early hour (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 3:25 AM).

Arrive 60–90 minutes early if flying today, especially if you don't have PreCheck. Very early morning departures (before 4 AM) may experience shorter waits, but mid-morning travel appears heavier. Standard TSA lines are significantly slower than PreCheck when available.
