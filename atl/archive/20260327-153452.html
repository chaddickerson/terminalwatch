Airport Update: ATL

TSA wait times at Atlanta Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport remain significantly elevated today. Early morning reports showed 58–70 minutes across all lines between 8:54 AM and 11:15 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 8:54 AM, Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 11:20 AM). However, recent traveler reports show significant variation. At 9:00 AM, a traveler experienced approximately 30–40 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:00 AM), while PreCheck passengers faced much longer delays—one traveler reported over 2 hours in the PreCheck line starting at 3:00 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:55 AM). By late morning, conditions improved noticeably; several travelers cleared security in 5 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 11:09 AM).

Arrive 2–3 hours early for domestic flights, particularly for early morning departures. PreCheck holders should plan for extended waits—expect 1.5 hours or more during peak hours. Mid-to-late morning conditions appear improving, suggesting shorter waits later in the day if your schedule permits.
