Airport Update: ATL

Today at ATL, travelers reported notably short security wait times throughout the morning. PreCheck wait times ranged from under 5 minutes to 20 minutes across multiple reports (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:03 AM, Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:20 AM, Bluesky - Mon Mar 30, 5:38 AM), with one traveler reporting only 8 minutes for PreCheck ID verification at South Domestic Gates, though X-ray scanning added approximately 10 additional minutes (Bluesky - Mon Mar 30, 9:19 AM). Regular security lines moved quickly as well, with reports of 5–15 minute waits throughout the morning (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:30 AM, Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:03 AM). One traveler cleared both baggage and TSA in under 8 minutes at 6 AM (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 8:59 AM). A morning report noted that bag check is the real bottleneck, not TSA itself (Bluesky - Mon Mar 30, 10:12 AM).

Arrive 30–45 minutes before your flight for standard security or PreCheck, prioritizing check-in and baggage drop as the longer step. CLEAR may provide additional speed if available.
