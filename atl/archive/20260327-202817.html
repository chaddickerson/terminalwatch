Airport Update: ATL

ATL security wait times today show extreme variability. Early morning experienced severe delays: a traveler entering the TSA line at 2:25 AM did not clear until 3:50 AM (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 6:24 AM), and TSA PreCheck took 1.5 hours (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 8:27 AM). Morning reporting showed just over 1 hour for regular security (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:37 AM).

Midday conditions improved dramatically. PreCheck had no wait around 1:36 PM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 1:36 PM), and multiple travelers reported clearing security in 5–12 minutes between 12 PM and 3 PM (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 2:40 PM; Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 2:48 PM; Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 3:51 PM). Most recent: a traveler arriving at 3:05 PM cleared in under 10 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 4:05 PM).

Recommendation: Afternoon and evening travelers should expect short waits; arriving 60–90 minutes before departure should suffice. Early morning departures (before 8 AM) require extra time—plan for 2+ hours before your flight, especially if checking bags.
