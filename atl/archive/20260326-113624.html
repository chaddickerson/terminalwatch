TSA PreCheck wait times at ATL today are severe. A traveler who started their PreCheck line in baggage claim at 4:45 AM cleared security at 6:15 AM, indicating a 1.5-hour wait, with reports that lines are likely growing to 2+ hours and extending out the door (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:24 AM). Another traveler reported their PreCheck wait at approximately 1.5 hours today, with no CLEAR or Touchless ID options available at the Domestic Security Checkpoint (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:58 AM). However, on the international side today, one traveler moved through security more quickly, entering the line at 4:40 AM and clearing by 5:20 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:53 AM).

Yesterday's PreCheck wait times at ATL varied considerably. One traveler waited 1 hour and 36 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 3:20 PM), while another cleared in 40 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 1:41 PM). Earlier yesterday, lines were shorter, with reports of PreCheck clearing in around 45 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 10:23 AM) and another traveler getting through in ~45 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:58 PM). One traveler also reported a 1-hour PreCheck wait yesterday evening (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 8:38 PM). An afternoon departure yesterday saw TSA taking about 30 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 3:38 PM).

General reports from yesterday noted that TSA lines wrapped through baggage claim and out the door, with one traveler in line outside the airport for a flight departing in 3.5 hours (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 8:47 AM). Another report indicated that an afternoon traveler who arrived at 12:23 PM for a 5:20 PM flight experienced 30 minutes through TSA (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 3:38 PM). One traveler who arrived 4 hours early yesterday had about 3 hours to spare after clearing security (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 4:21 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive at least 3 hours before your flight today. PreCheck lines are exceptionally long at 1.5–2+ hours, and CLEAR and Touchless ID are not currently available at the Domestic Security Checkpoint. If flying from the international side, conditions may be slightly faster. Avoid relying on PreCheck to speed up your journey significantly in current conditions.
