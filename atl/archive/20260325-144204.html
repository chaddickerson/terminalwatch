Recommendation: Plan to arrive 3 to 4 hours early if you have a morning flight at ATL today, as waits in the domestic terminal are averaging 74–83 minutes. If you have a TSA PreCheck or CLEAR membership, expect shorter waits—reports today show PreCheck lines clearing in 29–60 minutes compared to longer waits for regular lines—though PreCheck lines were also heavy earlier this morning at over 2 hours. If possible, book an afternoon or evening flight; travelers consistently report significantly faster processing times (20–30 minutes) during those periods. The international terminal is currently moving much faster, with minimal waits.
