TSA Watch: ATL

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 03:36 PM ET — 17 reports across 1 days

Mar 24: 3:35 PM|3:21 PM|2:34 PM

At the South Terminal, PreCheck was moving exceptionally fast today, with one traveler reporting no line at all at 1:30 PM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:09 PM). Earlier this morning, PreCheck lines were significantly backed up, starting in the loading/unloading zone at 6:41 AM (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 6:41 AM), though they began to ease by 7:10 AM (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 7:10 AM). One PreCheck traveler cleared security in just over 1 hour at 7:49 AM, with at least ten TSA agents working and Delta personnel assisting in the bin area (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 7:58 AM). However, general security lines moved faster than PreCheck during morning rush; one traveler in general security cleared before a PreCheck passenger even entered the security gates at 7:26 AM (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 7:28 AM).

General reports from across ATL today show significant variation in wait times. One traveler made it through TSA in just 2 minutes at mid-afternoon (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:27 PM), while another's wife waited 30 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:33 PM). A traveler at 1:23 PM reported getting through in about 1 hour despite long lines, describing them as orderly (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:23 PM). By early afternoon, multiple reports described the airport as practically empty (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 3:09 PM) and noted TSA lines had shrunk compared to the previous day (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 1:56 PM). Earlier this morning, a traveler experienced a total of 2 hours from arrival through screening with bag drop-off (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 6:35 AM). One report cited 30–40 minute wait times verified by independent observers, with ATL recommending passengers arrive 4 or more hours early (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:21 PM).

Given today's mixed reports, plan to arrive 2–3 hours early for domestic flights. Conditions have improved significantly since earlier in the week, but morning hours (especially 6–8 AM) saw heavier PreCheck congestion. If you have PreCheck, expect shorter waits, especially in the afternoon. General security lines may occasionally move faster during peak times. Afternoon travelers (after 1 PM) have seen substantially shorter waits.
