Airport Update: ATL

Atlanta airport security is experiencing significantly longer waits than normal today. Average wait times across all lines reached 70 minutes mid-morning, compared to a typical 14–19 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 09:24 AM). As of 09:37 AM, a traveler reported waiting just over an hour in regular security lines, with the queue extending outside the terminal (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 09:37 AM). Another traveler who entered the TSA PreCheck line at 2:25 AM did not clear until 3:50 AM—1 hour 25 minutes (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 06:24 AM). An earlier report documented 2.5+ hours for travelers who started their security process at 5:30 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 09:26 AM).

Plan to arrive at least 2 to 3 hours early for flights today at ATL. Standard PreCheck and regular lines are both severely affected. Early morning departures (5:30 AM onwards) have seen the longest delays; if you're traveling later in the day, conditions may improve, but expect significantly longer waits than usual.
