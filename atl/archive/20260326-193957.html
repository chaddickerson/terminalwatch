Airport Update: ATL

At Terminal Domestic – North, security wait times are elevated. A checkpoint reported 39 minutes earlier today, significantly above the typical 18 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 03:12 PM). However, one traveler who entered the North Main Checkpoint at 1:37 PM reported reaching their gate by 2:28 PM with lines moving steadily despite their length (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 03:10 PM).

At Terminal Domestic, wait times have fluctuated throughout the afternoon. Reports show 36–37 minutes between noon and 3 PM, compared to typical waits of 15–18 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 03:00 PM; Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:40 PM). PreCheck has seen mixed results: one traveler cleared in 25 minutes (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 11:28 AM), while another spent 2 hours in the PreCheck line (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 01:22 PM). Regular PreCheck averaged 30 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 02:16 PM), and touchless PreCheck was closed today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 02:16 PM).

Overall average wait times across all ATL lines ranged from 32–38 minutes today, roughly 16–27 minutes above normal (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 03:04 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 90 minutes before departure for domestic flights. PreCheck lines are unpredictable today—wait times have ranged from 25 minutes to 2 hours—so extra buffer time is warranted even with PreCheck status.
