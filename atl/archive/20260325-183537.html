Today at ATL, PreCheck wait times have varied significantly throughout the afternoon. At around 1 PM, PreCheck was approximately 50 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:19 PM), while another traveler reported a 75-minute wait in PreCheck (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:08 PM). However, other reports from earlier this afternoon show faster movement: PreCheck took 40 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 1:41 PM) and 45 minutes for both CLEAR and PreCheck combined (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:26 PM). One traveler noted that an international security line had only a 10-minute wait (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:19 PM).

Earlier today, reports showed mixed conditions. A traveler got through PreCheck in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:17 PM), and another cleared TSA in 10 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:27 AM). Morning reports indicated that lines were a lot shorter today with one person getting through in approximately 45 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 10:23 AM), and another clearing security in about 1 hour despite a long-looking line (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 9:54 AM). However, early this morning around 8:47 AM, one traveler reported the TSA line wrapped through baggage claim and out the door, with a departure time 3.5 hours away (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 8:47 AM).

Yesterday, March 24, PreCheck moved very quickly, with one traveler getting through in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:03 PM), and another reporting the same 5-minute TSA clearance time (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:10 AM). However, this contrasts with a Monday report of a 2-hour wait (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:51 AM).

Plan to arrive at least 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights at ATL today. PreCheck lines appear to range from 40 minutes to over 75 minutes depending on time of day, while regular security is seeing longer waits. If you have CLEAR, it does not appear to offer significant time savings currently—one report showed CLEAR and PreCheck taking roughly the same time. Arriving very early in the morning (before 8 AM) or later in the afternoon may offer better conditions, though today's afternoon has shown variable wait times.
