Airport Update: ATL

Security screening at ATL today has been significantly faster than recent days. Multiple travelers reported breaching security in 3 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:39 AM) and under 3 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:23 AM) at the Delta Domestic terminal earlier today. Other travelers cleared security in under 20 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 12:18 PM, Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 10:55 AM), with one report of 20 minutes in the PreCheck line (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 09:21 AM). One PreCheck traveler reported a much longer 3-hour wait (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 12:09 PM).

ICE agents are staffing ID checkpoints and assisting with bag screening, which appears to be accelerating throughput. An early morning traveler encountered a full PreCheck line extending to Carousel 2 at 5:55 AM, clearing by 6:35 AM (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 06:41 AM). Overall, wait times are trending downward.

Arrive 30–45 minutes early for domestic flights today. PreCheck and regular lines are both moving quickly, though one report suggests PreCheck occasionally runs longer than regular screening.
