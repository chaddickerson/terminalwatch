Airport Update: ATL

Atlanta airport security screening is experiencing longer-than-typical waits today, though conditions vary considerably by time and checkpoint. According to the most recent system-wide data, average wait times across all lines were 38 minutes as of midday, compared to a typical 14 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:30 PM). However, individual reports show significant variation.

TSA PreCheck waits have ranged from 25 to 45 minutes today. One traveler cleared PreCheck in 25 minutes (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 11:28 AM), while another reported 30 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:04 PM), and a third spent 43 minutes (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 10:26 AM). Standard security lines have been faster in some cases—one traveler reported just 5 minutes at the international terminal around 9:45 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:44 AM), while another waited 5 minutes in general security at 10 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:58 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive 60–75 minutes early for domestic flights, particularly if using standard security lines, though PreCheck lines may move slightly faster on average today.
