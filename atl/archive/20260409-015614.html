Airport Update: ATL

Security processing at ATL today has been notably quick. One traveler reported that security and customs clearance was fast and smooth (Twitter - Wed Apr 08, 09:37 PM). Earlier today, another passenger experienced a 10-minute security wait with border patrol agents checking IDs (Bluesky - Wed Apr 08, 12:36 AM). Recommendation: Current reports suggest arriving 30–45 minutes before your flight for standard security. PreCheck and CLEAR lines appear significantly faster; if you have PreCheck, you may need less time. Given today's generally smooth processing, ATL appears to be moving efficiently at the moment.
