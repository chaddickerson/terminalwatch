Recommendation: ATL wait times today are unpredictable and range widely depending on time of arrival and line type. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, expect 10–30 minutes on the faster end, though PreCheck lines have reportedly wrapped outside during peak morning hours. For regular security lines, plan for 45 minutes to over an hour. One frequent ATL flyer noted that afternoon and evening flights consistently see shorter waits (under an hour). Arriving 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights is safer if flying in the morning; if you can fly afternoons or evenings, waits are typically much lighter.
