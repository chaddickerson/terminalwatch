Recommendation: Plan to arrive at least 2.5 to 3 hours before your domestic flight at ATL. TSA PreCheck has seen mixed results—while some travelers cleared security in 40–45 minutes, others experienced waits of 1 hour or longer, so PreCheck is not guaranteed to be significantly faster right now. If you don't have PreCheck, allow even more buffer time given average waits exceeding 50 minutes.
