TSA Watch: ATL

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 02:35 PM ET — 25 reports across 1 days

Mar 24: 2:34 PM|1:59 PM|1:34 PM|12:37 PM|11:40 AM|11:17 AM|10:40 AM|9:57 AM|9:24 AM

Standard TSA lines at ATL averaged 35–40 minutes on Twitter (Tue Mar 24, 1:16 PM), which is reported as less than average. Priority/PreCheck wait times have varied significantly: one traveler cleared in 15 minutes through the priority line at noon (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 1:04 PM), while another reported over an hour for PreCheck at 7:49 AM (Bluesky: Tue Mar 24, 7:58 AM). A separate traveler spent 3 hours in the TSA PreCheck line on Mon Mar 23 (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 2:02 PM). One account noted that on the same morning, a friend who started at general security finished before the PreCheck traveler who began at 7:26 AM (Bluesky: Tue Mar 24, 7:28 AM).

Reports from earlier in the week showed much longer waits. One traveler reported a 9-hour line on an unspecified date, missing their flight (Bluesky: Tue Mar 24, 2:17 PM). Another experienced a 2-hour wait just to reach the TSA line on Wed Mar 19 (referenced in Bluesky: Tue Mar 24, 7:28 AM), with PreCheck suspended at that time. A traveler with an 8 PM flight on an unspecified date arrived at 3 PM and did not clear TSA until 7:30 PM (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 12:42 PM).

One positive account from Tue Mar 24 at 1:04 PM reported going through ATL expecting a 4-hour wait but clearing in 1.5 hours (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 1:04 PM). Another traveler on Tue Mar 24 at 1:23 PM noted that although lines were long, they got through in about an hour and found the process orderly (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 1:23 PM). A traveler arriving early morning on Tue Mar 24 spent approximately 2 hours total from arrival through screening, including bag drop-off (Bluesky: Tue Mar 24, 6:35 AM).

Based on current conditions, plan to arrive at least 2–3 hours early for domestic flights at ATL. Regular TSA lines are currently moving faster than they were earlier in the week (35–40 minutes vs. 3–9 hours previously), but PreCheck wait times remain unpredictable and have reached or exceeded 1 hour. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, expect similar or potentially longer waits than standard lines depending on the time of day. Arrive earliest if traveling between early morning and mid-afternoon, when demand appears highest.
