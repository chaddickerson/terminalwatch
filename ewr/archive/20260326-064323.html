How Early to Arrive

EWR has experienced significant volatility over the past two days, with waits ranging from 5 minutes to over 1.5 hours. Terminal A saw a particularly sharp increase mid-to-late afternoon on the 25th, with CLEAR and TSA PreCheck systems offline at one point. Early morning travel (before 9 a.m.) has consistently offered short waits—typically 5–10 minutes for TSA PreCheck and regular lines. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, plan to arrive 60–75 minutes before departure; if traveling with regular security, arrive 90–120 minutes early. Terminal C has generally shown faster processing than Terminal A, though conditions can change rapidly. Confirm current conditions closer to your departure time, as automated alerts may be suspended during peak stress periods.
