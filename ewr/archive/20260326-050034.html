A traveler who flew out of EWR earlier in the week reported 20-minute wait times (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 7:50 PM), and another who flew out Sunday experienced 20 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:23 PM).

Arrival Recommendations

EWR's wait times today have been unpredictable, ranging from under 5 minutes to over 1.5 hours. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, aim to arrive 45 minutes to 1 hour before departure, especially in the morning when lines are consistently lighter. For regular security, plan for 90 minutes to 2 hours, particularly during mid-day and afternoon periods. Terminal A and Terminal C have shown slightly faster PreCheck processing, so those with expedited programs may have better luck there. Early morning flights (5–8 a.m.) offer the most reliable, shortest waits across all terminals.
