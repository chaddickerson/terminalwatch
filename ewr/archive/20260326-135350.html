Airport Update: EWR

Terminal A: Early morning wait times at Terminal A ranged from short to moderate. TSA wait time was under 15 minutes at 4:18 AM, and TSA PreCheck showed short lines at 4:29 AM. However, by 6:25 AM, Terminal A was 25 minutes versus a typical 5 minutes. Later, TSA PreCheck was just 5 minutes at 8:00 AM.

Terminal B: Terminal B security moved very quickly, with a traveler clearing TSA in just 4 minutes at 7:19 AM.

Terminal C: TSA PreCheck combined with CLEAR was 8 minutes at 7:12 AM. Standard security reached 30 minutes at 7:19 AM. A PreCheck traveler reported roughly 10 minutes from shuttle arrival to Starbucks inside security after arriving at 7:00 AM.

General reports: Across all terminals, average wait times were 33 minutes at 6:24 AM, roughly 21 minutes above typical. One traveler reported TSA took only 9 minutes today.

Recommendation: Arrive 90 minutes early for domestic flights. PreCheck and CLEAR holders saw significantly faster processing; regular security lines were notably slower. Terminal B moved fastest; Terminals A and C showed more variable wait times.
