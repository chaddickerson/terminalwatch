Airport Update: EWR

Terminal B: Security lines were moving exceptionally fast earlier this evening, with a reported 1 minute wait time (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:14 PM).

Terminal A: Wait times have increased significantly during evening hours. TSA PreCheck was approaching 1+ hour as of 5:37 PM today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:37 PM). Earlier this morning, a traveler with PreCheck cleared in about 10 minutes after arriving at 7 AM (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 9:04 AM).

Terminal C: With CLEAR and TSA PreCheck, security took under 5 minutes this morning (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:32 AM). At 9:30 AM Wednesday, the line took 20 minutes (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 11:17 AM).

General reports: As of noon today, average waits across all terminals were 6 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:08 PM). By 5:41 PM, waits ranged from 3–20 minutes across terminals (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:45 PM). CLEAR and TSA PreCheck had a less than 60-second wait earlier this evening (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:14 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 90 minutes before departure for standard screening; 60 minutes if you have CLEAR or TSA PreCheck. Evening hours (after 5 PM) are seeing longer waits, particularly at Terminal A, so allow extra time for departures after rush hour.
