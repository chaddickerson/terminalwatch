Airport Update: EWR

Terminal A: Early morning offered quick PreCheck access—5 minutes at 5 a.m. (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 5:00 AM). Mid-day Wednesday saw around 30 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 5:11 PM).
