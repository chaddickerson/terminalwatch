Terminal A

A traveler with CLEAR and TSA PreCheck waited 1 hour to get through security today, arriving at 2:40 PM and clearing at 3:40 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:32 PM). However, earlier today at 8:45 AM, security moved much faster: CLEAR took not even 10 minutes while regular lines took 15 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:51 AM). At 5 a.m. today, Touchless TSA PreCheck was 5 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 5:00 AM). Mid-day today, one traveler reported 30 minutes for regular TSA (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 5:11 PM). Another traveler got through in 5 minutes today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:04 PM).

Terminal C

Today at 11:00 AM, a regular security line (non-PreCheck, non-CLEAR) cleared in just 38 seconds with only two people ahead (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:30 PM). Earlier today at 9:00 AM, TSA Pre Touchless took barely 3 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:22 AM). This morning, the line was moving at a good pace with plenty of TSA agents visible (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:14 PM). With Global Entry/PreCheck (no CLEAR), wait times were 5 minutes on a recent visit (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:38 PM).

General reports today indicate significant variability across EWR. Early afternoon TSA alerts reported average wait times of 30 minutes across all lines, compared to a typical 11 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:58 PM), though by late afternoon overall wait times spiked to 50 minutes average versus a typical 17 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:12 PM). However, many individual travelers reported quick passages: 20 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:08 PM), no line at TSA PreCheck at 1 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:11 PM), less than 5 minutes total (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:42 PM), and 15 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:48 PM). Early morning proved fastest: TSA PreCheck was empty on a 6 AM flight (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:31 PM), and 10 minutes with CLEAR and PreCheck on a 7 AM flight (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:33 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 60–75 minutes before departure, as afternoon wait times spiked to 50 minutes today. However, times vary considerably by terminal and time of day. Early morning flights (before 7 AM) appear fastest, especially in Terminal C where PreCheck lines have been nearly empty. In Terminal A, expect longer waits in the afternoon; CLEAR and PreCheck offer significant advantages (as short as 10 minutes) but even with these programs, afternoon waits can extend to 1 hour. If flying early morning, 45 minutes should suffice; for afternoon flights, aim for 75 minutes or more.
