Terminal A

Early this morning, Terminal A saw mixed conditions. TSA PreCheck with Touchless screening was under 15 minutes at 4:18 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:19 AM), and another traveler reported PreCheck was available and short (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:29 AM). However, wait times spiked midday yesterday: one traveler with CLEAR and TSA PreCheck waited a full 1 hour (arriving 2:40 PM, cleared by 3:40 PM) (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:32 PM), while another experienced approximately 30 minutes with PreCheck around mid-afternoon Wednesday (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 5:11 PM). Earlier in the week, PreCheck was 5 minutes at 5 AM on Wednesday (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 5:00 AM), and 5 minutes from curb to clear on Monday evening (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 6:27 PM).

Terminal B

Terminal B was nearly empty this morning. A traveler flying Spirit got through security in just 4 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:19 AM). Yesterday evening, regular lines moved in under 30 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:44 PM).

Terminal C

Terminal C showed strong performance early today. A traveler with both TSA PreCheck and CLEAR cleared in 8 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:12 AM). Earlier in the morning at 4:56 AM, PreCheck lines were posted at 6–10 minutes and moving at a decent pace, though visually intimidating (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:07 AM). Yesterday evening at 7 PM, PreCheck was fairly empty while regular security looked like approximately 30 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:33 PM). A traveler with Global Entry and PreCheck reported only 5 minutes earlier in the week (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:38 PM). On Monday morning, Touchless PreCheck was 5 minutes and regular security looked trivial (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:29 AM).

General airport-wide reports

A general traveler reported getting through security in approximately 20 minutes today, noting that lines moved quickly and efficiently (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:22 AM). Another traveler with TSA PreCheck Touchless got through in 8 minutes today, though noted that "all other lines look like a disaster" (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:46 AM). Automated alerts reported airport-wide average waits of 33 minutes (typical 12 minutes) early this morning (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:24 AM) and 25 minutes (typical 5 minutes) at 3:59 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:59 AM). Yesterday saw significantly longer waits, with airport-wide averages peaking at 1 hour 23 minutes in the early evening (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 7:12 PM). One journalist reported waits hit 1.5 hours with lines wrapping around the bag check area (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:59 PM). However, multiple travelers reported no significant delays this week, with times under 10 minutes or normal processing (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:21 PM), and one traveler noted EWR was running around 20 minutes average while other major airports faced far longer delays (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 6:10 AM).

Recommendation

Conditions at EWR remain variable. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, plan to arrive 30–45 minutes before your flight; early morning and Terminal B/C generally offer faster processing. For regular security lines, allow 60–90 minutes, especially during midday and afternoon hours when waits have exceeded typical times. Terminal C and Terminal A (early morning only) have shown better performance for PreCheck holders than regular lines, but delays yesterday in Terminal A with both PreCheck and CLEAR reached a full hour, so building in extra buffer time remains wise.
