As of Tuesday morning (Tue Mar 24, 10:16 AM), TSA wait times at EWR are running 25 minutes on average—about 16 minutes longer than typical. However, wait times vary significantly by terminal and time of day. Terminal A has seen notably shorter waits, with reports of less than 10 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 10:00 AM) and as low as 4–5 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 08:35 AM and 05:45 AM). Terminal C appears more congested based on traveler feedback.

Early morning offers the best conditions. Travelers passing through between 5–8 AM (Tue Mar 24, 03:44 AM – 08:35 AM) reported waits of 5–20 minutes in Terminal A. PreCheck and Touchless PreCheck lines move significantly faster—with reports of 5–16 minutes even during busier periods (Tue Mar 24, 05:21 AM and 06:03 AM). General security lines are slower, particularly in Terminal C.

Recommendation: Arrive at EWR at least 90 minutes before departure if flying from Terminal C, or 60 minutes if departing Terminal A with PreCheck. If you have PreCheck or Clear, use it—the time savings are substantial. Avoid midday travel if possible; early morning (before 8 AM) offers the smoothest experience based on recent reports.
