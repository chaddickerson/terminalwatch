Terminal B experienced severe congestion earlier today, with wait times reaching 83 minutes across all security lines—significantly above the typical 8 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM).

Terminal C

PreCheck performance at Terminal C has been mixed today. One traveler waited 13 minutes after dropping off at 1:40 PM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:57 PM), while another reported walking right through with no wait using Touchless ID earlier in the day (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:26 PM). On Monday morning, PreCheck moved smoothly in approximately 5 minutes, with regular security lines appearing light (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:29 AM).

Terminal A

Terminal A had quick processing earlier today. One traveler cleared security in 4 minutes and 23 seconds (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:35 AM), and another reported smooth operations with a brief wait on Monday evening when PreCheck took approximately 5 minutes from curb to TSA clearance (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 6:27 PM).

General Updates

Overall wait times at EWR have been volatile today. By late afternoon, average wait times across all lines climbed to 53 minutes—significantly above typical averages of 9–14 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:46 PM). Earlier today, a traveler reported waiting 3 hours in a PreCheck line (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:05 AM), though this appears to be an outlier. At midday, one traveler reported TSA wait times as low as 10 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:08 PM).

Recommendation

Conditions at EWR are highly variable today. Terminal A and Terminal C have shown faster processing, particularly for PreCheck travelers, while Terminal B has experienced extended delays. If flying today, plan to arrive at least 60–75 minutes before departure for domestic flights, especially if using Terminal B. PreCheck and CLEAR users should still allocate extra time but may move significantly faster than regular security lines—aim for 45 minutes if you have PreCheck. Touchless ID PreCheck at Terminal C has been particularly efficient when available.
