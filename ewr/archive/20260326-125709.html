Terminal A

Early today, TSA PreCheck lines at Terminal A were moving quickly. A traveler with Touchless TSA PreCheck cleared security in 5 minutes at 5 a.m. (Bluesky - Wed Mar 26, 5:00 AM), and another reported getting through in under 15 minutes at 4:18 a.m. (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:19 AM). Terminal B

Terminal B remained light on traffic today. One traveler reported clearing TSA in just 4 minutes flat during an early morning visit (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:19 AM). On Wednesday evening, another passenger reported lines moving as usual, under 30 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:44 PM).

Terminal C

Terminal C has shown consistently fast times today. A traveler with TSA PreCheck and CLEAR cleared security in just 8 minutes this morning (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:12 AM). An earlier visit at 4:56 a.m. reported PreCheck lines advertised at 6–10 minutes that were moving at a decent rate (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:07 AM). On Wednesday evening at 7 p.m., PreCheck was fairly empty and regular security looked like approximately 30 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:33 PM). Earlier in the week, security took less than 20 minutes at 9:30 a.m. (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:22 AM).

General Reports

One traveler reported that while lines appeared long this morning, they moved very quickly with no issues and were managed efficiently and cordially (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:22 AM). Another traveler with TSA PreCheck noted they were through in 8 minutes today, though all other lines appeared to be a disaster (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:46 AM). Automated alerts reported that average wait times across all lines ranged from 25 to 45 minutes throughout Wednesday afternoon and evening, compared to typical waits of 5–17 minutes—a difference of 20 to 36 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:25 AM). Wider reports from earlier in the week showed significant variation: one traveler reported no wait at TSA PreCheck at 1 p.m. on Wednesday (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:11 PM), while another cleared security in under 5 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:42 PM). Some travelers reported waits of around 20 minutes at EWR this week (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:17 PM), while others said times were normal (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:15 PM).

Recommendation

Terminal C and Terminal B are currently your fastest options, with early morning and off-peak times offering sub-10-minute waits for PreCheck and CLEAR holders. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, plan to arrive 15–20 minutes before your screening time, especially at Terminal C. For regular security lines, allow 30–45 minutes, particularly at Terminal A during mid-day hours. The most recent data shows Terminal C moving faster than other terminals, so choose that if your airline uses it.
