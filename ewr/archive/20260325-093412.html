Terminal A

TSA PreCheck moved quickly at Terminal A. A traveler with TSA PreCheck Touchless ID got through security in 5 minutes from curb to completion (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 6:27 PM). On Tuesday, wait times were 20 minutes in the afternoon, noted as slightly higher than normal (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 5:18 PM). One report described the line as essentially non-existent during the mid-afternoon (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:41 PM).

Terminal B

Terminal B experienced significantly longer delays. Wait times reached 83 minutes at midday on Tuesday (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM), which was 75 minutes longer than the typical 8-minute wait.

Terminal C

Terminal C showed mixed results. TSA PreCheck with Touchless ID moved exceptionally fast—one traveler cleared security in 5 minutes on Monday morning at 8 a.m. with regular security appearing trivial as well (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:29 AM). Another traveler with PreCheck took 13 minutes after dropping off at 1:40 p.m. on Tuesday (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:57 PM). A traveler using PreCheck with Touchless ID experienced no wait at all (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:26 PM). Earlier on Tuesday morning at 9:30 a.m., security took less than 20 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:22 AM).

General Reports (No Terminal Specified)

Airport-wide wait times on Tuesday varied significantly throughout the day. Mid-morning showed 25 minutes average across all lines (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:16 AM). By late morning, waits climbed to 56 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:19 PM). Throughout the early-to-mid afternoon, reports consistently showed 53 minutes across all lines (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:21 PM), Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:44 PM). By evening, conditions improved significantly, with travelers reporting quick and easy passage and wait times under 5 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:11 PM), (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:50 PM).

Arrival Recommendations

If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR with Touchless ID, plan to arrive 20–30 minutes before your flight's boarding time—recent reports show these expedited lanes are moving consistently fast, often under 15 minutes. If you're using regular security, plan to arrive at least 2 hours early, particularly if traveling through Terminal B, which has shown longer delays. Terminal C and Terminal A appear to have faster regular lines based on recent reports. Evening travel (after 6 p.m.) may offer shorter waits than midday hours.
