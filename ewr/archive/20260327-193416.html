Airport Update: EWR

Terminal A
TSA PreCheck lines at Terminal A have been consistently quick today. A traveler reported 10–15 minutes with PreCheck at 12:30 PM, noting the regular line was not significantly longer (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 12:55 PM). Earlier this morning, PreCheck was 10 minutes at 7:21 AM (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 7:21 AM), with regular security under 30 minutes.

Recommendation
