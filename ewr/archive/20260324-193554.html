TSA Watch: EWR

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 03:23 PM ET — 22 reports across 3 days

Mar 24: 3:21 PM|2:34 PM

Terminal C: Wait times at Terminal C have been highly variable today. One traveler cleared PreCheck in 13 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:57 PM), while another walked right through with no wait using Touchless ID earlier in the day (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:26 PM). Earlier today at 9:30 AM, security took less than 20 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:22 AM). On Monday morning, Touchless PreCheck moved through in just 5 minutes (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:29 AM).

Terminal B: Mid-day today, wait times spiked significantly, with current waits reaching 83 minutes—more than 75 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM).

Terminal A: Terminal A has experienced the shortest waits. This morning, one traveler cleared TSA in just 4 minutes 23 seconds (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:35 AM), and the morning experience was smooth overall (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:46 AM). At 10:00 AM today, waits were under 10 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:00 AM). On Monday evening, PreCheck moved through in 5 minutes from curb to security (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 6:27 PM).

General airport-wide data today shows significant delays. As of 2:46 PM today, average wait times across all lines were 53 minutes—about 44 minutes above typical (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:46 PM). Earlier today at noon, waits peaked at 56 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:19 PM). Morning conditions were better, with 25 minutes reported at 10:16 AM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:16 AM) and again at 9:00 AM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:00 AM). One traveler reported 30 minutes through regular TSA earlier today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:22 AM), though another reported waiting 3 hours in a PreCheck line this morning (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:05 AM).

Recommendation: Terminal A is your best option today, with waits consistently under 10 minutes. Plan to arrive at least 30–45 minutes before your flight if using Terminal B or C, where delays have been substantial this afternoon. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR access, use it—Touchless PreCheck has shown notably faster processing at Terminal C. Morning times (before 10 AM) were significantly better than afternoon/midday, so consider an early flight if possible.
