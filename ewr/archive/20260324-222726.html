Terminal A

Security moved quickly at Terminal A today. A traveler passed through in 20 minutes this afternoon, which was noted as slightly higher than normal (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 5:18 PM). Earlier today, TSA PreCheck was extremely fast at 5 minutes from curb to security (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 6:27 PM).

Terminal B

Terminal B experienced significantly longer waits today. At noon, security wait times reached 83 minutes, which was 75 minutes longer than the typical 8-minute wait (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM).

Terminal C

Terminal C showed mixed results today. TSA PreCheck moved quickly—a traveler was through in approximately 13 minutes after drop-off at 1:40 PM, though they expressed frustration with the timing (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:57 PM). Earlier this morning, security was under 20 minutes at 9:30 AM (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:22 AM). On Monday morning, TSA PreCheck (Touchless ID) was exceptionally fast at just 5 minutes, with regular security also looking minimal (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:29 AM).

General Reports (All Terminals)

Airport-wide wait times escalated significantly throughout today. By early afternoon, average waits across all lines reached 53 minutes (reported consistently between 12:21 PM and 2:46 PM), compared to typical waits of 9–14 minutes—a 39–47 minute increase (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:46 PM). This was the worst surge of the day. Earlier around noon, airport-wide waits were 56 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:19 PM). By mid-morning, conditions were improving—waits were 25 minutes at 10:16 AM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:16 AM). One traveler reported roughly 30 minutes through TSA earlier today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:22 AM). A TSA PreCheck traveler who flew last Tuesday reported breeze-through times of 10 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 4:20 PM).

Recommendation

Plan to arrive 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights today at EWR, based on afternoon wait times averaging 50+ minutes across the airport. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, conditions are notably better—Terminal C PreCheck traffic moved under 20 minutes this morning, and Terminal A PreCheck has been consistently fast. However, Terminal B showed extreme congestion at midday (83 minutes), so if departing from Terminal B, add extra buffer time or consider using PreCheck/CLEAR if available. Airport-wide conditions have been highly volatile today, so arriving on the early end of the 2–2.5 hour window is safer than cutting it close.
