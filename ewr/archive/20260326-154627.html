Airport Update: EWR

Terminal A: TSA PreCheck wait times have been notably short today. At 4:18 AM, PreCheck was under 15 minutes, and at 8:00 AM, PreCheck showed 5 minutes. However, earlier alerts indicated longer waits: at 6:25 AM, standard security averaged 25 minutes (typical: 5 minutes).

Terminal C: Wait times vary significantly by line type. At 4:56 AM, PreCheck was moving at a decent rate despite appearing intimidating. At 7:20 AM, standard security took 30 minutes, but PreCheck with CLEAR was only 8 minutes. At 11:26 AM, one traveler reported 7 minutes today. An earlier report noted that at 9:30 AM, standard security took 20 minutes.

General reports: Several travelers reported fast overall experiences. One noted 10–15 minutes at EWR earlier this week. Another reported 8 minutes this morning, up from their usual 4 minutes. A TravelQ sensor alert at 10:26 AM indicated average wait times across all lines were 23 minutes (typical: 7 minutes).

Recommendation: Arrive 60–75 minutes early for domestic flights. PreCheck and CLEAR holders should see significantly faster processing (5–15 minutes), while standard security lines warrant the full buffer, especially Terminal C.
