Recommendation

Terminal C and Terminal A have shown consistently fast PreCheck processing, with most reports showing 5–15 minutes30–45 minutes before departure if you have PreCheck or CLEAR. Regular security lines vary more widely—Terminal C saw 30 minutes in evening hours while some reports show much faster times—so arrive 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes early for standard screening. Terminal A showed one outlier report of 1 hour total for CLEAR + PreCheck users during afternoon hours (2:40 p.m.), suggesting midday travel may require more buffer; if flying then, plan an extra 15–20 minutes. Overall, Newark has handled recent demand well, but afternoon hours appear busier than early morning.
