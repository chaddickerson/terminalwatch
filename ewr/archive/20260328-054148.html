Airport Update: EWR

Terminal A: PreCheck wait times have ranged from 5–15 minutes during early morning hours (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 5:00 AM). Around midday yesterday, PreCheck was approximately 10 minutes with regular lines under 30 minutes (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 12:55 PM). Early morning yesterday showed 10 minutes for PreCheck (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 7:21 AM).

Terminal C: This morning yesterday, regular security was 41 minutes at 5 a.m. (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 5:34 AM). With TSA PreCheck and CLEAR combined, wait times were as short as 8 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 7:27 AM).

General reports: Yesterday evening, wait times across all terminals ranged from 1–20 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 7:04 PM). PreCheck alone was reported at just 5–10 minutes during midday hours (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 11:08 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive 60–90 minutes before departure for regular security; PreCheck and CLEAR holders can arrive closer to 45 minutes before departure. Terminal C saw longer regular lines in early morning yesterday, so consider additional buffer time if flying early.
