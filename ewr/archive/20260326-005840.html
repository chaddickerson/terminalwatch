Recommendation

EWR has experienced highly variable wait times throughout today, ranging from minutes to over an hour and a half. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, especially at Terminal C, arrive 2 hours before departure—PreCheck lines have been consistently fast (often under 15 minutes). For regular security lines, particularly in Terminal A where delays peaked at 1 hour with PreCheck and CLEAR combined, arrive 3 hours early. Terminal C appears to have faster and more consistent throughput than Terminal A. Early morning flights (before 8 a.m.) show the shortest waits across all line types.
