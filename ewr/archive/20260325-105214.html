General (All Terminals Combined)

Earlier on Tuesday morning, waits were more modest at 25 minutes above typical levels around 10:16 AM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:16 AM). Recommendation

Conditions appear to be normalizing. TSA PreCheck with Touchless ID remains your best option at EWR, especially in Terminals A and C, where PreCheck travelers are seeing 5–13 minutes even during busy periods. Terminal B experienced much longer delays mid-day on Tuesday, so consider checking real-time conditions before heading to that terminal. For today and going forward, plan to arrive at least 90 minutes before departure if using regular security, and 60 minutes if you have TSA PreCheck.
