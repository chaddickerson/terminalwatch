TSA Watch: EWR

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 02:01 PM ET — 26 reports across 3 days

Mar 24: 1:59 PM|1:34 PM|12:37 PM|11:40 AM|11:17 AM|10:40 AM|9:57 AM|9:24 AM

TSA wait times at Newark (EWR) spiked significantly on Tuesday afternoon. Average waits across all terminals reached 56 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 12:19 PM), well above the typical 9-minute average. Terminal B was particularly congested at 83 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM), though general airport-wide alerts showed 25-minute averages earlier in the day (Tue Mar 24, 6:54 AM through 10:16 AM).

Performance varied significantly by terminal and time. Early morning (Tue Mar 24, 3:44 AM–8:35 AM) saw much faster processing, with reports of 5 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 3:44 AM), 4 minutes 23 seconds at Terminal A (Tue Mar 24, 8:35 AM), and less than 20 minutes at Terminal C (Tue Mar 24, 10:22 AM). TSA PreCheck lines were significantly faster, with travelers reporting 13 minutes from curb to cleared security at Terminal A with PreCheck (Tue Mar 24, 5:21 AM) and walked right through with no wait at Terminal C on PreCheck (Tue Mar 24, 1:26 PM).

If traveling through EWR, arrive at least 90 minutes early for afternoon/evening flights, especially at Terminal B. Early morning departures (before 9 AM) have shown significantly shorter waits. TSA PreCheck and Touchless ID check-in both report substantially faster processing times across all terminals.
