Airport Update: EWR

TSA PreCheck and CLEAR lines at EWR are moving exceptionally fast today. At Terminal 3, a traveler cleared the PreCheck Touchless ID process in approximately 5 minutes with no wait time (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:19 PM). Terminal C saw similarly quick times: one traveler with CLEAR and PreCheck completed check-in and security in under 5 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:32 AM), and another reported a 20-minute wait at 9:30 a.m. (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 11:17 AM).

General reports confirm minimal congestion across terminals today. The average wait time across all EWR terminals was just 6 minutes at noon (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 12:08 PM). Multiple travelers reported no wait or minimal delays during morning hours (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:08 PM), and even one traveler's usual 4-minute wait doubled to 8 minutes this morning (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:42 AM).

Arrive 15–20 minutes early if you have PreCheck or CLEAR; regular security lines may require 20–30 minutes. Conditions are unusually quiet today across all terminals.
