Airport Update: EWR

Terminal C: Security lines were averaging 15–30 minutes for regular passengers this morning, with PreCheck at approximately 15 minutes (Bluesky - Sat Apr 04, 6:25 AM). Earlier yesterday, one traveler reported no TSA line for PreCheck in the morning, though the regular line appeared short as well (Twitter - Sat Apr 04, 12:02 PM).

Terminal B: Wait times were 10–15 minutes for regular security lines, with PreCheck moving faster at 5–10 minutes (Bluesky - Sat Apr 04, 6:25 AM).

Terminal A: Security lines were under 10 minutes, with PreCheck under 5 minutes (Bluesky - Sat Apr 04, 6:25 AM).

General observations: Evening and night hours show significantly shorter waits—averaging just 4 minutes between 9 PM and 4 AM, compared to 14 minutes in the afternoon (Twitter - Sat Apr 04, 6:37 AM). One traveler cleared security in just 9 minutes yesterday evening (Twitter - Sat Apr 04, 10:11 PM).

Recommendation: Terminal A offers the shortest waits; Terminal C is slowest. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, arrive 15 minutes before your security screening. Regular passengers should budget 30 minutes at Terminal C, or 20 minutes at Terminals A and B. Flying late night (after 9 PM) will save significant time.
