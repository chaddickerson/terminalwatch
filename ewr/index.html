As of Tuesday, Mar 24, 9:00 AM ET, TSA wait times at Newark are running 25 minutes across all lines—about 18 minutes longer than typical. This represents the most current official report. Earlier reports show similar patterns. Recommendation: Plan to arrive at least 45 minutes before domestic departure if using general security, or 30 minutes if you have PreCheck. Early morning departures (before 7 AM) showed shorter waits in reports from Tuesday. If available, use PreCheck or touchless options—they consistently cut wait times in half. Times vary significantly by terminal and time of day, so build in extra buffer during peak hours.
