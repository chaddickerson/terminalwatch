Mar 24: 2:34 PM

Terminal B
Wait times at Terminal B spiked significantly today. TSA reported 83 minutes of wait time across all lines (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:03 PM), which was 75 minutes longer than typical.

Terminal C
Terminal C has shown mixed conditions today. One traveler using PreCheck reported waiting 13 minutes after dropping off at 1:40 PM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:57 PM). Earlier, another traveler with Touchless ID PreCheck walked right through with no wait (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:26 PM). On Monday morning at 8 AM, Touchless PreCheck moved passengers through in 5 minutes flat, while regular security looked trivial (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 8:29 AM). This morning at 9:30 AM, travelers got through in less than 20 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:22 AM).

Terminal A
Terminal A has been moving quickly today. One traveler cleared security in 4 minutes and 23 seconds this morning (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:35 AM). Another reported a smooth experience this morning (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:46 AM). At 10 AM, Terminal A had less than 10 minutes of wait time (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:00 AM). On Monday evening, one PreCheck traveler cleared security in 5 minutes from curb to TSA (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 6:27 PM).

General Airport-Wide Reports
Overall wait times across all terminals today have been elevated. TSA reported average waits of 53 minutes across all lines at 2:46 PM today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:46 PM), 44 minutes longer than typical. Earlier in the afternoon at 12:19 PM, the average was 56 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:19 PM). Mid-morning reports showed 25 minutes average wait time (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:16 AM) and 25 minutes at 10 AM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:05 AM). Early morning at 9 AM, waits averaged 25 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:00 AM), and at 6:54 AM, 25 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 6:54 AM). One traveler reported a 3-hour wait in a TSA PreCheck line this morning (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:05 AM), though another reported 10 minutes earlier today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 11:22 AM). One traveler recommended arriving early and using Touchless TSA PreCheck (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 5:23 AM).

Recommendation
Conditions at EWR are highly variable today. Terminal A and early-morning Terminal C with PreCheck are your fastest options, with some travelers clearing security in under 5 minutes. Terminal B is significantly congested with 83-minute waits. If you must travel today, arrive at least 90 minutes early for Terminal B, and 60 minutes early for other terminals. Using TSA PreCheck—especially Touchless ID where available—offers a significant advantage, with some travelers experiencing minimal to no waits on PreCheck lines while regular lines show much longer times.
