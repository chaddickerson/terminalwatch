Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 09:15 AM ET — 5 reports across 3 days

Wait times at EWR have improved significantly since the beginning of the week. As of Tuesday morning (Tue Mar 24, 10:10 AM), average wait times were around 20 minutes across the airport. This represents a major improvement from the severe congestion reported just days earlier.
