TSA Watch: BOS

Last updated: Mar 26, 2026 07:33 AM ET — 17 reports across 4 days

Mar 26: 6:36 AM|6:02 AM

Terminal A
TSA PreCheck at Terminal A was 4 minutes early this morning when a traveler arrived at 5:45 AM for a 7:45 AM flight, with the normal security line also appearing very manageable (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:07 AM). Earlier, around 8:45–9:00 AM on Tuesday, PreCheck had no line and the regular PreCheck line had approximately 8 people (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:32 PM).

Terminal B
TSA at Terminal B cleared travelers in 5 minutes this morning (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:07 AM). On Tuesday at 9:30 AM, the security line was essentially nonexistent (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:06 PM). An earlier report noted the line took 6 minutes and 53 seconds to clear, with notably friendly TSA staff (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 9:48 AM). One traveler joked that the Dunkin line was longer than TSA (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 4:52 AM).

Terminal E
Staff at Terminal E encouraged checked baggage, which resulted in unusually fast TSA processing (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 10:07 PM).

General Reports
A traveler returning from Boston on Tuesday experienced a 5-minute wait (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:34 PM). No lines were reported on Tuesday (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:14 PM). One traveler noted that arriving very early allowed them to walk straight through security, with Boston praised as "the best place" for TSA efficiency (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:05 PM). A TSA PreCheck holder reported waiting no more than 5 minutes across four security visits during recent trips to Boston and other airports (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 10:54 PM). Monday morning reports described TSA as normal with friendly staff (Bluesky - Mon Mar 23, 11:24 AM), and another traveler noted a ~5 minute wait at what appears to be a major hub (Twitter - Mon Mar 23, 10:53 AM).

Recommendation
Based on recent reports, BOS is currently experiencing very short TSA wait times across terminals. Arriving 90 minutes before departure should be sufficient for most travelers, even without PreCheck. PreCheck holders should plan for 60–75 minutes
