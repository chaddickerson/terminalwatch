Airport Update: BOS

Security screening at Boston Logan Airport today is moving exceptionally fast. A traveler reported essentially no wait in TSA PreCheck lines earlier this evening (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 08:15 PM). Earlier today, wait times were minimal across the airport: one traveler cleared security in just 5 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 04:31 PM), another in under 15 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 06:51 AM), and a third in just over 20 minutes even during what they described as the longest line they'd seen (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 06:40 AM). Another traveler confirmed they walked right in with minimal delay (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 04:48 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 30–45 minutes before domestic flights. Current conditions at Boston Logan are significantly better than many other major U.S. airports, and PreCheck lines in particular are moving very quickly.
