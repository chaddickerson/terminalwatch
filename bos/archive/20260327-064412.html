Airport Update: BOS

PreCheck lines at BOS have been consistently fast. A traveler reported 7 minutes through PreCheck (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 8:55 PM), and another experienced less than 10 minutes at the checkpoint overall (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 4:33 PM). Regular security also moved quickly—one traveler cleared TSA in 10 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 3:30 PM).

Terminal A saw virtually no wait in the TSA PreCheck line around 8:45 AM, with only about 8 people in the regular PreCheck queue (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:32 PM).

Terminal B had nonexistent lines at 9:30 AM (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:06 PM), with one traveler clearing security in under 7 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 9:48 AM).

Recommendation: Based on recent data, arriving 30–45 minutes before departure should suffice at BOS, even without PreCheck. PreCheck holders can arrive even closer to departure time given typical waits under 10 minutes.
