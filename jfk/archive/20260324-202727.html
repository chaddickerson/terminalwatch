TSA Watch: JFK

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 03:36 PM ET — 12 reports across 1 days

Mar 24: 3:35 PM|3:21 PM|2:34 PM

Terminal 4
PreCheck with CLEAR got through in approximately 20 minutes today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:24 PM). Standard PreCheck without CLEAR was approximately 30 minutes today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:24 PM). The regular security line took 51 minutes earlier today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:34 PM).

Terminal 5
Security wait times were significantly elevated at 42 minutes today, compared to a typical 21 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:05 PM). One traveler reported PreCheck wait times in excess of one hour (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:47 PM).

Terminal 7
Security screening was running at 29 minutes today, significantly above the typical 13–14 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:03 PM).

Terminal 8
PreCheck moved quickly at under 4 minutes earlier today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:47 PM).

One traveler reported the TSA app was showing inaccurate wait times this morning, initially displaying 15–30 minutes but resulting in a wait of over 2 hours (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:09 AM). However, another traveler who dropped off passengers today noted that TSA lines "were not bad at all" with a total wait of just under an hour for check-in and security combined (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:25 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights today. If you have PreCheck and are flying out of Terminal 4 or 8, you can likely cut this to 1.5 hours. Terminal 5 appears to have the longest waits, even for PreCheck; add extra buffer time there. CLEAR members at Terminal 4 saw the fastest processing at around 20 minutes, making it worthwhile if available.
