Terminal 5

TSA PreCheck at Terminal 5 experienced severe delays today. A traveler with TSA PreCheck reported it took 3.5 hours from arrival to clearing security, with baggage drop-off alone taking over an hour (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:05 AM). Another PreCheck passenger reported waiting 1 hour to get through the PreCheck line, when it normally takes 10 minutes on a Wednesday morning; the regular line was extremely long (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:45 AM). Early morning arrivals (5:00 AM) did not avoid delays (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:03 AM). However, one traveler at Terminal 5 reported passing through security in just 20 minutes at 11:15 AM today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:31 AM).

Terminal 8

General security lines at Terminal 8 were very long and wrapped around the entry hall as of 11:15 AM today. However, TSA PreCheck moved much faster, taking approximately 10 minutes to get through (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:15 AM).

Terminal 4

TSA PreCheck at Terminal 4 had a 10–20 minute wait at 10:50 AM today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:53 AM).

General Reports (All Terminals)

JFK as a whole is experiencing significantly longer than usual TSA wait times today. The average across all lines was 64 minutes as of 11:01 AM, compared to a typical average of 13 minutes—a 51-minute increase (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:01 AM). JFK, along with LGA and EWR, has suspended live wait time reporting as of mid-morning today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 11:23 AM). Reports indicate wait times of 2+ hours in some cases across all three airports. One traveler using both TSA PreCheck and CLEAR waited only 2 minutes early this morning around 5:30 AM, though regular lines were significantly longer (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:07 AM).

Recommendation

Plan to arrive significantly earlier than usual—conditions today suggest adding at least 1.5 to 2 hours before your flight. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, expect substantially shorter waits, though even PreCheck lines at Terminal 5 have exceeded 1 hour today. Regular security lines are experiencing severe delays, with some reports of 2+ hour waits. Terminal 8's PreCheck line (~10 minutes) and Terminal 4's PreCheck line (10–20 minutes) appear to be moving faster than Terminal 5, though conditions are changing rapidly throughout the day.
