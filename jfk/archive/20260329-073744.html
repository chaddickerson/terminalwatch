Terminal 4: Wait times spiked dramatically early today. At 2:56 AM, TSA reported a 105-minute wait at Terminal 4, compared to a typical 4-minute baseline (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 2:56 AM).

Terminal 5: Conditions were similarly severe. At 2:50 AM, Terminal 5 posted a 105-minute wait against a normal 42-minute average (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 2:50 AM).

Airport-wide: TSA reported the most recent average across all terminals at 105 minutes today at 2:53 AM, compared to typical 8-minute waits (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 2:53 AM). Recommendation: Arrive at least 3 hours early today, especially for early-morning flights. PreCheck and CLEAR holders should not assume faster processing—both are experiencing significant delays. Terminal 4 and Terminal 5 face the worst congestion.
