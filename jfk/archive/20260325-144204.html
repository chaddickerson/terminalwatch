Terminal 5

Today around 6:45 AM, TSA PreCheck took 1 hour 5 minutes, while the premium security line took 55 minutes for one traveler's friend (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:36 AM). Another traveler reported that TSA PreCheck at Terminal 5 took 1 hour for an early morning departure, compared to a normal wait of about 10 minutes on a Wednesday (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:45 AM). A separate report noted that even with TSA PreCheck, the wait was 3 1/2 hours from arrival to clearing security, with baggage drop-off alone taking over 1 hour (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:03 AM). Real-time airport data today indicated TSA lines at Terminal 5 were running 45–65+ minutes compared to a normal 10–15 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:45 AM). Standard security lines at Terminal 5 were reported as extremely long today.

Terminal 7

As of this morning, TSA PreCheck at Terminal 7 was only 1 minute, while the regular line was around 20 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:31 AM).

General Reports (Non-Terminal-Specific)

Overall TSA wait times at JFK today were significantly elevated, with average wait times across all lines at 57 minutes compared to a typical 11 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 9:00 AM). One traveler reported waiting 3 hours in security and missing a connecting flight (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:13 AM). However, a traveler with TSA PreCheck and CLEAR cleared security in just 2 minutes around 5:30 AM today, while "the other line wasn't as kind" (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:07 AM). One traveler who arrived over 3 hours early got through security in under 20 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:32 AM). Another report from earlier today noted general wait times around 30 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:25 AM). Early this morning at 5 AM, one traveler reported a 2+ hour line and described the scene as chaotic (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:03 AM).

Recommendation

Plan to arrive at least 3–4 hours early for domestic flights today, especially at Terminal 5, where PreCheck and regular lines are both experiencing severe delays. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, you should see faster processing, though Terminal 5 PreCheck is still running 1+ hours today. Terminal 7 offers notably shorter wait times. Arriving very early (5–6 AM for morning flights) may help you avoid peak congestion.
