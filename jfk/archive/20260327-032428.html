Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 4 showed mixed conditions today. Earlier, at 7:00 PM, another traveler using PreCheck got through in less than 10 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 9:35 PM). Recommendation: Arrive 3 hours early for domestic flights. Terminal 4 PreCheck holders may clear security faster in evening hours, but morning and early-evening peak times show unpredictable delays across all terminals and line types.
