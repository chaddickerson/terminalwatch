Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5 is experiencing severe delays today. PreCheck and standard lines both showed 133 minutes as of mid-morning (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 10:12 AM), about 121 minutes longer than typical. One traveler reported a 2-hour PreCheck wait on Thursday and still made their 7:40 AM flight after arriving at 5:30 AM (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 10:08 AM).

Terminal 8 is also significantly delayed. TSA PreCheck lines hit 83 minutes around 10:21 AM today (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 10:21 AM), more than 66 minutes above normal. Standard security was 55 minutes earlier this morning (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:34 AM).

Terminal 7 is moving better, with 20 minutes from the AirTrain through security as of 10:30 AM today (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 10:30 AM).

Across JFK overall, average wait times are 43 minutes, roughly 27 minutes above typical (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:34 AM). One traveler reported standard lines approaching 2+ hours with PreCheck around 45+ minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 9:55 AM).

Arrive 3+ hours early for domestic flights today, especially for Terminals 5 and 8. Terminal 7 appears faster and may require less buffer time. PreCheck offers some advantage but still expect significant waits.
