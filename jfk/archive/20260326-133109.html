Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 8

PreCheck lines at Terminal 8 have been exceptionally long today. One traveler reported waiting about 1 hour at 6:30 AM before clearing by 7:35 AM (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 7:41 AM). Another reported 43 minutes just to reach the PreCheck corral at 8:07 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 8:07 AM). A traveler arriving at 7:15 AM found the PreCheck line entrance poorly marked, with confused passengers and significant delays (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:39 AM).

Terminal 5

One PreCheck traveler arriving at 4:00 AM today waited 2 hours 30 minutes to clear security, exiting just after 6:30 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:23 AM). The same traveler noted that regular lines were significantly more congested, with some travelers cutting in line, though TSA agents remained helpful. By Wednesday evening, PreCheck at Terminal 5 was moving much faster at 12 minutes (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 8:55 PM).

Terminal 4

Terminal 4 experienced elevated wait times throughout the morning. At 7:05 AM, wait times were 38 minutes above typical (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:05 AM), and at 6:13 AM they were similarly at 38 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:13 AM). A traveler who arrived at 3:45 AM reported waiting 55–60 minutes for security, praising TSA staff efficiency despite the long queue (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:57 AM).

General Reports

Across all JFK terminals, security wait times have been significantly elevated today. At 8:29 AM, average wait times were 59 minutes against a typical average of 11 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 8:29 AM). A PreCheck traveler reported waiting 1.5 hours at an unspecified terminal earlier today (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 8:41 AM). Another traveler reported a 2.5-hour security line at an unspecified terminal (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 7:47 AM), and another experienced a 2-hour wait with the line extending outside the building (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 5:58 AM). At 6:00 AM, PreCheck alone was running 90 minutes versus a typical 13 minutes (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 6:00 AM). One traveler who arrived at 4:00 AM waited 1 hour 50 minutes even with early arrival and relatively fast movement (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:00 AM). Another arrived at 4:00 AM and only cleared security after 6:30 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:53 AM), with the line extending outside the terminal.

Recommendation

Plan to arrive at least 3–4 hours before your departure today. Conditions are unusually severe across all terminals. If you have PreCheck or CLEAR, expect waits of 45 minutes to 1.5 hours rather than the typical 10–15 minutes. Regular security lines are running 2+ hours. Even arriving very early (4:00 AM for an 8:00 AM flight) may leave little margin. Consider checking in online and arriving with maximum buffer time.
