Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 4: Most recently, PreCheck took approximately 20 minutes today, while PreCheck with CLEAR was around 5 minutes, and regular TSA security was approximately 1 hour (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 4:43 PM). Earlier today at 11:00 AM, the general security line took about 15 minutes (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 11:46 AM). However, morning reports indicated severe congestion, with PreCheck around 45 minutes at 6:49 AM (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 6:49 AM).

Terminal 8: PreCheck took 44 minutes today at 4:24 PM (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 4:24 PM). This morning, conditions were much worse: PreCheck was 1 hour 40 minutes at 8:43 AM (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 8:43 AM), and 1 hour 15 minutes at 7:29 AM (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 7:29 AM). Early morning (7:15 AM) saw 50-minute PreCheck waits (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 12:02 PM).

Terminal 7: Wait times were approximately 20–25 minutes at noon today (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 12:59 PM).

Terminal 5: PreCheck took 43 minutes earlier today (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 8:12 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 3–4 hours early if traveling without checked luggage, or 4–5 hours with a bag. TSA PreCheck and CLEAR significantly reduce wait times but don't eliminate them—even combined they took 45 minutes today at some terminals. Terminal 4 and 7 currently show faster processing than Terminal 8.
