TSA security lines at JFK are experiencing severe delays across multiple terminals this morning (Tue Mar 24). TSA PreCheck lines are moving faster than standard security lines. One traveler with PreCheck and Clear reported clearing Terminal 4 in 10 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 09:14 AM), while another made it through a PreCheck line in under 10 minutes (Tue Mar 24, 09:03 AM). If traveling through JFK today, arrive at least 3–4 hours early for domestic flights and plan accordingly for international departures. Consider using TSA PreCheck or CLEAR if available, as these lines are processing passengers significantly faster than standard security. Check directly with your airline for any flight-specific updates.
