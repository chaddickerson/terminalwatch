Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 4 showed wide variation today. At 10:00 AM, regular security lines were manageable (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 02:56 PM). Mid-morning around 11:00 AM, the security line was short and efficient (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 11:00 AM). However, conditions deteriorated significantly by evening: a traveler who entered the security line at 4:00 PM waited 3 hours, with the line wrapping around the entire terminal (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 08:52 PM). Later at 7:00 PM, PreCheck moved much faster, with one traveler clearing security in under 10 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 09:35 PM).

Terminal 8 saw substantial PreCheck delays this morning. At 6:30 AM, the TSA PreCheck line took approximately 1 hour to clear (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 07:41 AM).

Earlier today, one traveler reported a 90-minute wait in the TSA PreCheck line at JFK, though the specific terminal wasn't identified (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 07:20 PM). A separate report mentioned a 2.5-hour security line today, also without terminal specifics (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 07:47 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive 3+ hours early for afternoon/evening flights at Terminal 4, where lines were significantly longer between 4:00–5:00 PM. PreCheck offers faster processing but experienced delays this morning at Terminal 8. Morning flights (before 10:00 AM) may face heavier congestion.
