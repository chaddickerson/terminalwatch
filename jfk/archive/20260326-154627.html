A traveler reported 39 minutes through security with TSA PreCheck at 8:47 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 11:06 AM).

Terminal 5: One traveler reported a 2 hour 30 minute wait, arriving at 6:45 AM and clearing security at 9:15 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:59 AM).

Overall, JFK is experiencing longer-than-usual waits today. Current average wait times across all lines are 70 minutes, compared to a typical 12 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 10:31 AM). Arrive at least 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights. TSA PreCheck and CLEAR provide significant advantages at Terminal 4 (3–20 minutes), but Terminal 8 PreCheck lines are substantially longer (54 minutes to 1+ hour). Regular lines at Terminal 8 without expedited screening exceed 1 hour.
