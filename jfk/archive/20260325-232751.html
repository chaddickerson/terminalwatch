Terminal 4

Terminal 4 is experiencing severe delays today. Wait times reached 83 minutes in the afternoon (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:00 PM), compared to a typical 21 minutes. Earlier in the day, Terminal 4 also reported 83-minute waits (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:00 PM).

General Reports (All Terminals)

Airport-wide, TSA delays intensified significantly through the afternoon today. The average wait across all lines reached 55 minutes by early evening, about 38 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:01 PM). Earlier today, the average was 67 minutes at 3:59 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:59 PM) and 64 minutes at 1:58 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:58 PM).

Individual travelers reported extended waits today. One passenger waited 2.5 hours in the TSA line before finally getting through after 3 hours total (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:41 PM). Another reported being in line for 2.5 hours with conditions described as chaotic (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:55 PM). Early-morning travelers also faced 2+ hour waits at 5:00 AM (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:03 AM).

Not all reports reflected today's delays. A traveler with TSA PreCheck experienced a quick 12-minute wait yesterday morning, and noted regular security lines appeared normal (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:17 PM). Another passenger from a few days ago cleared regular TSA in 30 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:43 PM).

Recommendations

Plan to arrive at JFK at least 5 hours before your boarding time today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:34 PM). Terminal 4 in particular is experiencing extended delays, and conditions have been chaotic with multiple security lines. TSA PreCheck does not appear to provide a significant advantage under today's conditions; even PreCheck lines have backed up substantially.
