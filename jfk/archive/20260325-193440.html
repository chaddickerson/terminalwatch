Terminal 4

TSA wait times at Terminal 4 spiked significantly today. At 1:16 PM, regular security lines reached 83 minutes, compared to a typical wait of 23 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:16 PM). However, TSA PreCheck moved much faster—a traveler cleared security in just 10 minutes at around the same time (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:22 PM). Earlier in the morning, a TSA PreCheck passenger walked through with no line whatsoever at 10:30 AM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:00 PM).

Terminal 5

A JetBlue traveler at Terminal 5 reported very slow-moving security at around 11:40 AM today, with very few conveyor belts open, though they acknowledged the situation was manageable (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 12:13 PM).

Airport-Wide Reports

Across all terminals at JFK, average wait times climbed to 64 minutes by 1:58 PM today, compared to a typical average of 23 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:58 PM). Earlier readings at 1:04 PM showed 44 minutes average wait across all lines (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:04 PM).

However, individual experiences varied widely. TSA PreCheck passengers reported notably shorter waits: one traveler cleared in 5 minutes at 11 AM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:02 PM), while another reported less than 10 minutes on a recent trip (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:07 PM). A passenger reported waiting approximately 45 minutes in regular security (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:16 PM). Early morning security was significantly worse—one traveler encountered 2+ hour waits at 5 AM (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:03 AM).

A traveler who departed yesterday morning reported PreCheck took 12 minutes and regular lines appeared normal (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:17 PM).

Recommendation

If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, plan to arrive 2–2.5 hours before departure; PreCheck lines are running significantly faster than regular security today. If you're in a regular line, especially at Terminal 4, plan for 3+ hours early arrival, particularly during midday hours. Avoid early morning (5–6 AM) if possible, as severe delays have been reported. Terminal 4 in the afternoon showed the longest regular-line waits, so if you're departing from there without PreCheck, allow extra cushion time.
