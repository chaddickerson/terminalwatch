Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 4 showed mixed results today. The most recent report from this evening indicates 1 hour 10 minutes wait time to clear TSA (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:37 PM). Earlier in the afternoon, a traveler reported that regular security lines were manageable around 10 a.m., though they noted lines vary throughout the day (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 2:56 PM). Mid-morning, another traveler found the security line short and efficient (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 11:00 AM).

Terminal 8 experienced significant delays in PreCheck lines this morning. TSA PreCheck wait time was approximately 1 hour at 6:30 a.m., described as the longest one traveler had seen there (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 7:41 AM).

One traveler noted that PreCheck moved at similar speeds to regular lines, with CLEAR passengers moving noticeably faster (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 7:33 AM).

Recommendation: Terminal 4 appears more manageable than Terminal 8 today. Arrive 2–3 hours early for standard lines; PreCheck holders should plan 1.5–2 hours, especially during early morning and evening peaks. CLEAR significantly reduces wait times if available.
