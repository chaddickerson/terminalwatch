Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5 saw longer-than-typical waits today. Earlier this afternoon, TSA wait times reached 56 minutes around 11:43 AM (Twitter - Thu Apr 02, 11:43 AM), which is 43 minutes above the typical 13-minute wait. By late morning, waits had improved slightly to 31 minutes (Twitter - Thu Apr 02, 11:58 AM).

Terminal 4 showed mixed results today. A traveler in the regular security line (without PreCheck or CLEAR) waited approximately 30 minutes after arriving around 11 AM (Twitter - Thu Apr 02, 01:20 PM). Earlier in the morning, the line was 20 minutes (Twitter - Thu Apr 02, 07:27 AM).

Early morning reports suggest faster processing. PreCheck wait times were under 10 minutes during the 7 AM hour (Twitter - Thu Apr 02, 07:58 AM), and one traveler cleared security in 15 minutes today (Twitter - Thu Apr 02, 04:14 PM).

Recommendation: Plan for 30–45 minutes at Terminal 5 if traveling this evening. Terminal 4 regular lines should take around 25–35 minutes; PreCheck may be faster. Earlier morning departures (before 8 AM) appear significantly quicker across terminals.
