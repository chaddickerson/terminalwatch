Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5 is experiencing significantly elevated wait times today. At 12:19 PM, wait times were 62 minutes—more than triple the typical 18-minute average (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 12:19 PM). By 1:01 PM, times had improved slightly to 47 minutes, still 23 minutes above normal (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 1:01 PM). However, PreCheck fared much better: one traveler passed through PreCheck by 1:00 PM after arriving at 12:30 PM, while regular security passengers who finished around the same time had waited approximately 3 hours (from 10:00 AM–1:00 PM) (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 1:20 PM). Priority Security had a 40-minute wait (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 1:20 PM).

Terminal 8 reported a 3-hour security line at 10:00 AM today (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 1:16 PM).

Across all JFK terminals, average wait times were 28 minutes at 11:40 AM, compared to a typical 12 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 11:40 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive at least 3–4 hours early, especially at Terminal 5 for regular security. TSA PreCheck offers significantly shorter waits today.
