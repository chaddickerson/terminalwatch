TSA Watch: JFK

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 05:27 PM ET — 11 reports across 1 days

Mar 24: 5:25 PM|4:27 PM|3:35 PM|3:21 PM|2:34 PM

Terminal 5: Regular security lines were running 42 minutes today, significantly above the typical 21 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:05 PM). Earlier today, a traveler reported waiting over 2 hours in security, though they noted the TSA wait time app may not have been displaying accurate information due to recent shutdowns (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:09 AM).

Terminal 7: Regular security wait times were 29 minutes today, about 15–16 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:03 PM).

Terminal 4: PreCheck and CLEAR combined allowed travelers through in approximately 20 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:24 PM), and a separate PreCheck traveler reported clearing security in roughly 30 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:24 PM). However, the Delta PreCheck line in Terminal 4 was reported as unusually crowded earlier today (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 4:38 PM).

Terminal 8: Alaska Airlines PreCheck moved very quickly, with one traveler getting through in under 4 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:47 PM).

One traveler reported that regular security at JFK took just under 1 hour total, and noted that it is still wise to arrive early despite some social media reports exaggerating conditions (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:25 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights at JFK today. Terminal 5 is experiencing the longest delays; if you have PreCheck or CLEAR, you can expect significantly faster service (20–30 minutes) in Terminals 4 and 8, though Terminal 4's Delta PreCheck line has been congested. Regular security lines are running 30–40+ minutes above normal across multiple terminals.
