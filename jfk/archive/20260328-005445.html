Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5: Conditions have been severe today. Early morning saw 5+ hours in the security line (Bluesky - Fri Mar 27, 02:38 PM). Later, TSA PreCheck wait was around 20 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 05:25 PM), though the regular line appeared long. A traveler reported the JetBlue line was extremely congested with no CLEAR option available, recommending at least 2 hours in line (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 07:45 PM).

Terminal 8: Current wait was 39 minutes as of early evening (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 05:55 PM), significantly above the typical 20 minutes.

Terminal 4: Expect 35 minutes currently, up from a typical 18 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 06:04 PM). Earlier reports flagged 2+ hours of pre-TSA congestion (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 07:38 PM).

Terminal 7: 27 minutes reported, above the typical 8 minutes (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 07:09 PM).

Overall, TSA PreCheck lines ranged from 20–25 minutes in some reports (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 06:13 PM) to 90 minutes and beyond in others. Arrive at least 2–3 hours early for domestic flights, especially at Terminal 5. PreCheck and CLEAR may offer modest relief but are not guaranteed.
