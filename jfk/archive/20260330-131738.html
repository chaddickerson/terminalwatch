Airport Update: JFK

Today at JFK, wait times vary significantly by terminal and service level. Terminal 8 showed 40 minutes for general security as of early morning (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:14 AM), though TSA touchless lines moved under 10 minutes while main security took around 1 hour (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:35 AM). Terminal 5 reported 47 minutes typical wait (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 5:26 AM), and Terminal 4 reached 54 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:37 AM). Across all JFK terminals, average wait time was 36 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:16 AM), about 16 minutes above typical. A traveler with PreCheck and CLEAR got through in about 20 minutes (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 8:37 AM), though another traveler without expedited services waited 1 hour 50 minutes at Terminal 5 around 6 AM (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 7:35 AM).

Plan an extra 45 minutes to 1 hour beyond typical arrival time, especially for regular security lines. PreCheck and CLEAR significantly reduce waits—consider these if available. Terminal 8's touchless option is fastest if you qualify.
