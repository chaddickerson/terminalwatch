Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 8 experienced severe delays today. PreCheck wait times ranged from 43 minutes to over 1 hour early this morning (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 07:41 AM), with one traveler reporting 54 minutes through PreCheck as recently as 9:46 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 09:46 AM). Regular security in Terminal 8 was significantly worse—one traveler without PreCheck or CLEAR waited 1 hour 13 minutes after arriving at 7:10 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 09:38 AM)—and another reported 1 hour 15 minutes total, cautioning against the ICE agent line (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 09:40 AM).

Terminal 4 showed mixed conditions. With CLEAR and PreCheck, wait times were minimal—as short as 4 minutes at 9:30 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 09:37 AM). However, without premium services, wait times climbed to 38 minutes at 9:10 AM, significantly above the typical 7 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 09:10 AM).

Terminal 5 also faced extended waits—one PreCheck traveler required 2 hours 30 minutes after arriving at 4:00 AM (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 07:23 AM).

At 8:29 AM, average wait times across all JFK terminals were 59 minutes, nearly five times the typical 11 minutes (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 08:29 AM).

Recommendation: Terminal 4 offers the fastest PreCheck/CLEAR service today. If flying without premium security, plan an extra 60–90 minutes; Terminal 8 regular lines are particularly congested. PreCheck and CLEAR provide substantial time savings across all terminals.
