General reports today suggest much faster waits. One traveler cleared security at JFK in under 15 minutes on a recent trip (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 2:42 PM), and another got through in under 10 minutes (Bluesky - Sun Mar 29, 2:03 PM).

Recommendation: Terminal 4 is moving exceptionally well today—arrive 2 hours before departure. For other terminals, aim for 2.5–3 hours early, especially without TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, as Sunday data shows regular lines moved considerably slower than expedited options.
