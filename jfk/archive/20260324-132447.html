TSA Watch: JFK

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 09:14 AM ET — 4 reports across 2 days

Early morning remains the most problematic time at JFK. Terminal 5 experienced severe congestion around 6:30 AM (Tue Mar 24, 6:30 AM), with disorganized lines, unclear queue starts, and reports of passengers missing flights. More recent reports indicate waits exceeded 1 hour at JFK overall (Tue Mar 24, 10:03 AM).

Terminal 8 shows a stark divide between PreCheck and general screening. At Terminal 8 (Mon Mar 23, 10:31 PM), PreCheck and touchless ID lanes moved smoothly, while standard security lines faced significant delays. If you have PreCheck, you'll experience substantially faster processing.

Arrive 2.5 to 3 hours early for early morning flights (before 8 AM) if you're in a standard line, especially at Terminal 5. If you have PreCheck, plan for 1.5 hours at Terminal 8. Avoid the 6:30 AM window if possible, and consider applying for PreCheck given the current disparity in wait times across terminals.
