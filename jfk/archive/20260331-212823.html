Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5
TSA PreCheck experienced significant delays today, with a wait of 58 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 2:47 PM), compared to a typical 25 minutes. General security lines were shorter at 41 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 2:47 PM), though still elevated from the usual 25 minutes. An earlier report from this morning indicated exceptionally long lines, with queues looping through multiple floors (Bluesky - Tue Mar 31, 8:11 AM).

Terminal 4
General security reported 61 minutes today (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 1:45 PM), compared to a typical 32 minutes. An earlier report from midday showed 49 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 1:10 PM).

One traveler noted that posted TSA wait times on the JFK website this morning were not accurate, and that lines were particularly brutal in the early morning but improved as the day progressed (Twitter - Tue Mar 31, 11:31 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive at least 2.5 to 3 hours early for domestic flights. Terminal 4 and Terminal 5 are experiencing above-average wait times; PreCheck lines at Terminal 5 are particularly slow today, so standard security may be faster.
