TSA Watch: JFK

Last updated: Mar 24, 2026 02:51 PM ET — 13 reports across 1 days

Mar 24: 2:34 PM

Terminal 5: Wait times have been significantly elevated today. A traveler reported 1 hour 38 minutes in the PreCheck line earlier this morning (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:10 PM). Around 6:30 AM, conditions were chaotic with very long lines and disorganization, and multiple travelers missed their flights (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 8:33 AM). By mid-afternoon, the general security line was running 42 minutes, about 21 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:05 PM).

Terminal 7: General security lines were 29 minutes as of early afternoon, 15 minutes above the typical 14-minute wait (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:25 PM).

Terminal 4: A traveler using CLEAR and PreCheck got through in 20 minutes today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:24 PM). Earlier in the day, the regular security line took 51 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:34 PM).

Terminal 8: A PreCheck traveler got through in under 4 minutes this afternoon (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:47 PM).

One traveler reported that after dropping family at JFK earlier today, regular security took just under an hour, which they characterized as "not bad" (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 1:25 PM). However, another traveler's TSA app estimated 15–30 minutes but the actual wait turned out to be over 2 hours earlier today (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 10:09 AM). Airline staff at JFK mentioned travelers should expect around 2 hours for TSA during current conditions (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 12:02 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive at least 2–2.5 hours early for domestic flights today. Terminal 4 and Terminal 8 appear to have better throughput than Terminal 5, particularly if you have PreCheck or CLEAR. If you have neither, Terminal 5 should be avoided if possible—regular security there is experiencing significant delays. PreCheck and CLEAR holders should see substantially shorter waits (20 minutes or less in most terminals), making those programs especially valuable at JFK today.
