Airport Update: JFK

Early today, the Clear/TSA PreCheck line at JFK was exceptionally crowded, with one traveler reporting the line was 20 times longer than usual at 5:30 AM, though most passengers were arriving 2–5 hours before their flights (Twitter - Sun Mar 29, 12:32 AM).

Terminal 4: Security moved quickly yesterday evening, with one traveler using CLEAR completing check-in and security in 45 minutes for a 7:15 PM flight (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 9:32 PM), and another reporting just a 10-minute TSA line at 10:24 PM (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 10:24 PM).

Terminal 5: TSA PreCheck travelers reported excellent conditions, with waits of 5 minutes and 15 minutes in evening hours (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 7:20 PM; Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 4:50 PM), though an earlier morning report noted 45 minutes with PreCheck at 5:45 AM (Bluesky - Sat Mar 28, 12:15 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive 2–3 hours early for domestic flights. Terminal 4 and 5 showed faster processing than system-wide averages, but PreCheck holders saw notably shorter waits than regular security lines across terminals.
