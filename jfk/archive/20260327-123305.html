Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5: Wait times are significantly elevated today. The average across all security lines stands at 60 minutes, compared to a typical 26 minutes—a +34 minute difference (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 08:22 AM). One traveler reported security lines extending over 3 hours earlier this morning (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 08:02 AM). Another noted arriving at 4 AM for an 8 AM flight found the line already extending out the door (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 08:19 AM).

Terminal 4: TSA PreCheck and CLEAR lines are moving faster than regular security. A traveler reported 20 minutes in the TSA PreCheck touchless line, with CLEAR appearing even faster, while regular TSA was heavily backed up (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 07:30 AM).

General airport conditions: Across all JFK terminals, average wait times are 39 minutes versus a typical 15 minutes—a +24 minute difference today (Twitter - Fri Mar 27, 08:25 AM).

Recommendation: Arrive at least 3 hours early for domestic flights, particularly at Terminal 5. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, those lines are moving noticeably faster than regular security.
