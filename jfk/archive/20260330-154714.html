Airport Update: JFK

Today at JFK, wait times show significant variation by terminal and service level. At Terminal 4, security wait times were 54 minutes as of early morning, about 26 minutes longer than typical (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:37 AM). A traveler with PreCheck and CLEAR reported getting through in about 20 minutes earlier today (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 8:37 AM). However, a Friday afternoon report from Terminal 4 noted standard lines reached 4 hours, with Delta Sky Priority TSA at 1.5 hours (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 10:34 AM).

At Terminal 8, conditions were more favorable. TSA Touchless cleared in under 10 minutes, while PreCheck and business/priority lines were comparable, and main security took about 1 hour as of 6:00 AM (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:35 AM). At Terminal 5, one traveler cleared security in about 10 minutes using TSA's disability assistance program (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 11:05 AM).

Across all terminals, average wait times were 36 minutes, 16 minutes above typical (Twitter - Mon Mar 30, 6:16 AM). Arrive 2–3 hours early for standard security; PreCheck and CLEAR holders should budget 1–1.5 hours. Terminal 8 with TSA Touchless is currently your fastest option.
