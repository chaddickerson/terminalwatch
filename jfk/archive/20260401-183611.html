Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5: TSA wait times were 41 minutes today, exceeding the typical 23 minutes by 18 minutes (Twitter - Wed Apr 01, 01:14 PM). Earlier today, the security line was significantly longer than expected (Bluesky - Tue Mar 31, 08:11 AM).

Terminal 7: Wait times reached 26 minutes this morning, about 20 minutes above the typical 6 minutes (Twitter - Wed Apr 01, 10:01 AM).

Terminal 8: TSA PreCheck moved quickly at 5 minutes early this morning at 7:10 AM, with ICE (Immigration and Customs Enforcement) assisting TSA staff (Twitter - Wed Apr 01, 07:37 AM).

Terminal 4: Security was notably faster than usual this morning (Bluesky - Wed Apr 01, 08:01 AM), with TSA processing taking less than 15 minutes for some travelers, including curbside bag drop (Twitter - Wed Apr 01, 04:58 AM).

General conditions today show mixed results. Mid-afternoon waits across JFK and Newark are running 40+ minutes, though early morning waits have improved significantly from typical peak-hour delays (Twitter - Wed Apr 01, 02:14 PM). Many travelers reported smooth processing, with some clearing security in 5–15 minutes.

Recommendation: Arrive 90–120 minutes before domestic flights and 2–3 hours before international flights. PreCheck holders are experiencing faster processing; if you have PreCheck or CLEAR, expect substantially shorter waits, particularly in Terminals 8 and 4.
