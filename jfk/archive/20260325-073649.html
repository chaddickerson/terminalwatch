At Terminal 8, a traveler with TSA PreCheck and CLEAR reported waiting approximately 25 minutes in the PreCheck line, though the regular line appeared significantly longer (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:29 PM).

At Terminal 4, TSA PreCheck wait times were under 15 minutes, but the regular security line was notably long (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:05 PM).

One traveler reported extreme crowding in the TSA PreCheck line for Delta at JFK, describing it as extending through half the terminal (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 4:38 PM).

A non-terminal-specific report from earlier on Mar 24 indicated a traveler waiting 2 hours at JFK with over 300 people in line and only 2 TSA agents staffing the checkpoint (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:55 PM). Predicted live wait data for JFK showed typical times around 13 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 1:09 PM).

Recommendation: Arrive at least 3–4 hours early for domestic flights. TSA PreCheck or CLEAR holders should experience significantly faster processing—typically under 30 minutes—but regular security lines are experiencing substantial delays. Terminal 4 appears slightly faster than Terminal 8 for PreCheck, but delays are widespread. If you don't have PreCheck or CLEAR, plan for the longest possible wait times.
