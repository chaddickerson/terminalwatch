Recommendation: Wait times at JFK are highly variable today—TSA PreCheck holders are seeing 5–12 minute waits, but regular security lines are experiencing 45 minutes to 3 hours. If you have TSA PreCheck, arriving 90 minutes before departure should be adequate. If you do not have PreCheck or CLEAR, allow 3+ hours to account for extended regular line waits, particularly during peak travel times (early morning and afternoon). Terminal 4 appears to have faster PreCheck processing based on today's reports.
