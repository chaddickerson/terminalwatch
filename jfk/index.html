At Terminal 4, TSA PreCheck moved quickly today, with a traveler clearing security in less than 15 minutes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:05 PM). However, the regular security line was notably congested, suggesting a significant gap between PreCheck and standard processing times at that terminal.

Terminal 5 had only 3 of 16 TSA security bays staffed today, though lines were not extending outside the checkpoint area (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:27 PM).

At Delta's terminal, the TSA PreCheck line was reported to be exceptionally crowded, extending across roughly half the terminal (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 4:38 PM).

Earlier today, overall JFK TSA wait times were running at 13 minutes on average, with typical peak waits around 20 minutes and quieter periods closer to 8 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 1:09 PM).

Recommendation: If you have TSA PreCheck, plan to arrive 30 minutes before departure, especially at Terminal 4 where PreCheck lines have been faster. Without PreCheck, arrive significantly earlier—at least 60–90 minutes before departure—as regular lines have shown substantial delays throughout the day, particularly at Delta's terminal.
