Terminal 4

Terminal 4 has been experiencing severe delays today. At 4:00 PM, wait times reached 83 minutes, compared to a typical 21 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:00 PM). Earlier in the afternoon at 2:00 PM, Terminal 4 also reported 83-minute waits against a typical 24 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 2:00 PM). However, TSA PreCheck passengers have moved through Terminal 4 much faster—one traveler cleared security in just 10 minutes with PreCheck today (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:22 PM).

General (All Terminals)

Wait times across JFK have been consistently elevated today. The most recent airport-wide report at 6:01 PM shows current average waits of 55 minutes compared to a typical 17 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:01 PM). At 3:59 PM, the average across all lines was 67 minutes against a typical 19 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:59 PM). An earlier 1:58 PM report noted 64-minute waits versus typical 23 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 1:58 PM).

Individual traveler reports paint a picture of extreme congestion today. One passenger reported waiting 2.5 hours in line and still waiting at 4:55 PM (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:55 PM), while another took 3 hours to clear security (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:41 PM). Early morning conditions were also problematic—at 5:00 AM, travelers encountered 2+ hour waits at Terminal 4 (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:03 AM).

However, conditions have not been uniformly severe. A TSA PreCheck passenger reported clearing security in 12 minutes yesterday morning (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 3:17 PM), and another traveler made it through regular security in 20–25 minutes a few days ago (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:37 PM).

Recommendation

Plan to arrive at least 5 hours before your boarding time today given current conditions (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:34 PM). Terminal 4 is currently the most affected terminal, with 80+ minute waits in regular lines. If you have TSA PreCheck, expect significantly faster processing—around 10 minutes—making PreCheck substantially more valuable today. Conditions have been severe throughout the day, including early morning hours, so plan accordingly regardless of departure time.
