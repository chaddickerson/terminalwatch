Terminal 7

General security lines at Terminal 7 were running 29 minutes (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 2:25 PM ET), which is 15 minutes longer than the typical 14-minute wait.

Terminal 5

At Tue Mar 24, 1:05 PM ET, general security waits reached 42 minutes, compared to a typical 21 minutes (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 1:05 PM ET)—a difference of +21 minutes. Earlier in the morning around 6:30 AM, Terminal 5 experienced severe congestion with disorganized lines and reports of passengers missing flights (Bluesky: Tue Mar 24, 8:33 AM ET). However, one traveler reported that TSA PreCheck took 1 hour and 38 minutes at Terminal 5 around 6 AM, allowing her daughter to make a 9 AM flight (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 12:10 PM ET).

Terminal 4

At Tue Mar 24, 2:24 PM ET, a traveler with CLEAR and TSA PreCheck got through in just 20 minutes (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 2:24 PM ET). General security in Terminal 4 took 51 minutes at midday (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 12:34 PM ET).

General Reports (Non-Terminal-Specific)

One traveler reported that general TSA screening at JFK took just under 1 hour on Tue Mar 24, 1:25 PM ET and described the lines as "not bad" (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 1:25 PM ET). Overall airport-wide waits ranged from approximately 20 to 90 minutes across terminals on Tue Mar 24, 1:30 PM ET, according to tracker data, though conditions vary significantly by terminal and time of day (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 1:30 PM ET). The TSA mobile app has been providing inaccurate wait estimates; one traveler was told to expect 15–30 minutes but experienced over 2 hours (Bluesky: Tue Mar 24, 10:09 AM ET). Staff advised that typical TSA processing at JFK is taking around 2 hours due to current staffing constraints (Twitter: Tue Mar 24, 12:02 PM ET).

Recommendations

Arrive at least 3 hours early for domestic flights and 4 hours early for international flights. Terminal 4 with CLEAR and TSA PreCheck showed significantly faster processing (20 minutes), so enrollment in these programs is valuable. Terminal 5 experienced more severe delays and disorganization, particularly in early morning hours, so allow extra buffer time there. General security lines without PreCheck or CLEAR are currently running 40–50+ minutes across terminals, well above typical. Do not rely on the TSA mobile app for accurate wait time estimates at this time.
