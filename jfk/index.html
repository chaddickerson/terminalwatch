General reports today indicate that JFK experienced significant TSA congestion. According to airport tracking data, wait times across JFK were 13 minutes, with predicted busiest times around 20 minutes and quietest times around 8 minutes (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 1:09 PM). However, multiple reports today described the situation as "insane" with lines described as overwhelming and potentially unsafe (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:44 PM), (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 3:31 PM). The disruptions have been attributed to staffing shortages related to a partial government shutdown (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 4:36 PM). One contrasting report suggested that wait times at JFK were "hardly any" today (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 2:57 PM), indicating variability depending on time and terminal.

Recommendation: If you have TSA PreCheck, plan to arrive 30–45 minutes before your flight's departure time. If you're traveling with a regular ticket, arrive significantly earlier—consider 2+ hours given today's reports of extended lines and crowding, particularly at Terminal 4 and for Delta flights. CLEAR status would likely improve your time. Consider using Terminal 7 if possible, as its wait times today were more moderate than other terminals. Avoid peak times (around 20 minutes of predicted busy periods) if your schedule allows.
