Airport Update: JFK

Terminal 5 is experiencing severe delays today. The most recent report shows 66 minutes of wait time (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 08:05 AM), though an earlier reading at 7:30 AM indicated 116 minutes (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 07:30 AM). One traveler observed PreCheck snaking back 500+ people at 5:40 AM (Bluesky - Sat Mar 28, 05:40 AM).

Terminal 4 shows lighter delays, with 29 minutes current wait time (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 08:00 AM).

Across all JFK terminals, average wait times stand at 56 minutes (Twitter - Sat Mar 28, 08:15 AM), well above typical Saturday levels.

Recommendation: Arrive at least 2–2.5 hours before departure, especially for Terminal 5 and Terminal 8. PreCheck holders should expect 45–75 minutes; regular passengers may face 2+ hours. Terminal 4 is currently the faster option if available.
