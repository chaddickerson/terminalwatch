Terminal 5: PreCheck was moving significantly faster than the regular line today (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 8:55 PM). A traveler with TSA PreCheck waited approximately 12 minutes, while the regular line on the adjacent checkpoint was notably longer.

Airport-wide conditions today: Overall wait times at JFK have been significantly elevated. As of this evening, average wait times across all security lines were 56 minutes, compared to a typical average of 12 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 8:02 PM). Earlier in the evening, the average was 55 minutes versus a typical 17 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 6:01 PM). A traveler reported waiting 2 or more hours in the TSA line at 5:00 AM this morning during what they described as chaotic conditions (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 7:03 AM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive significantly earlier than usual—at least 2.5 to 3 hours before your domestic flight and 3 to 4 hours before international departure given today's severe delays. If you have TSA PreCheck, you will see considerably faster processing; Terminal 5 data suggests 12-minute waits for PreCheck versus much longer regular lines. CLEAR membership would provide an additional advantage if available at your terminal.
