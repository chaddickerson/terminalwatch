Terminal 5

TSA PreCheck at Terminal 5 was 12 minutes earlier this week (Bluesky - Wed Mar 25, 8:55 PM). However, today a traveler with TSA PreCheck who arrived at 4:00 AM did not clear security until just after 6:30 AM — a wait of over 2.5 hours — though the traveler noted that TSA agents remained calm and helpful despite heavy passenger congestion and line-cutting (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:23 AM).

Terminal 4

Today's wait times at Terminal 4 have been significantly elevated. At 6:13 AM, TSA reported a 38-minute wait (typical: 17 minutes) (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:13 AM), and by 7:23 AM wait times remained at 38 minutes (typical: 12 minutes) (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 7:05 AM). One traveler who checked in around 3:45 AM reported a 55–60 minute wait in the security line (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 5:57 AM).

General Reports (All Terminals)

Today's early morning saw severe congestion across JFK. At 6:04 AM, TSA reported an average wait of 52 minutes across all terminals (typical: 14 minutes) (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:04 AM), and by 6:27 AM this had risen to 55 minutes (typical: 15 minutes) (Twitter - Thu Mar 26, 6:27 AM). One traveler reported that the TSA line wrapped outside the terminal building with a 2-hour wait (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 5:58 AM), while another reported TSA PreCheck alone at 90 minutes (normally 13 minutes) (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 6:00 AM). Early this morning, PreCheck and regular security lines moved at roughly the same pace, with CLEAR users receiving priority service (Bluesky - Thu Mar 26, 7:33 AM).

Earlier in the week, conditions were more variable. On Monday, a traveler experienced a 45-minute wait with TSA PreCheck but 2.5 hours without it (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 10:34 PM).

Recommendation

Plan to arrive at least 4 hours early for international flights today, particularly at Terminal 5, where early-morning waits have exceeded 2.5 hours even with TSA PreCheck. At Terminal 4, expect 55–60 minute waits even for early morning departures. If you have TSA PreCheck, expect significant delays today — the advantage over regular security appears minimal. CLEAR members are currently receiving priority access. Early morning (before 5:00 AM) and mid-afternoon (around 5:00 PM) are reported as the most congested periods at JFK.
