A traveler with TSA PreCheck and CLEAR at Terminal 8 reported wait times of approximately 25 minutes in the PreCheck line, though the regular security lines appeared significantly busier (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 9:29 PM).

At Terminal 5, only 3 of 16 TSA security bays were open, resulting in long lines, though they were not extending outside the terminal (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 7:27 PM).

The TSA PreCheck line for Delta at JFK was reported to be extremely congested, extending roughly halfway across the terminal (Bluesky - Tue Mar 24, 4:38 PM).

Across all lines at JFK, average wait times today are running 83 minutes, compared to a typical average of 11 minutes—a difference of +72 minutes (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 4:58 AM). One traveler reported waiting 1.5 hours in a regular security line after arriving at 2:30 a.m. (Twitter - Wed Mar 25, 5:38 AM). Another traveler cited a 2-hour wait with only two TSA agents staffing the checkpoint and additional agents leaving due to shift changes (Twitter - Tue Mar 24, 8:55 PM).

Recommendation: Plan to arrive significantly earlier than usual—at least 3 to 4 hours before departure today. If you have TSA PreCheck or CLEAR, you may see somewhat faster processing (25 minutes reported at Terminal 8), but even PreCheck lines are experiencing delays. Regular security lines are severely congested and should be avoided if possible.
