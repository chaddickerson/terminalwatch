PreCheck lines snaked back approximately 500 people at 5:40 AM, with general security appearing significantly worse (Bluesky - Sat Mar 28, 5:40 AM). Recommendation: Plan to arrive 2.5–3 hours early for domestic flights today, particularly at Terminal 8. Regular security lines are moving considerably slower than PreCheck; if available, PreCheck offers meaningful time savings despite current delays.
